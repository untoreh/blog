izindlela zokuphila

Ukuhlukaniswa kwezindlela zokuphila

Lokhu kungachazwa njengobubanzi phakathi kwabakhi (abafuduki) nabahambi (imihambima).

Abakhi bakhetha ukuma ndawonye nokuthi buthelela value ngesimo sawo esihlala njalo, babheka amasistimu futhi bakhe ukuxhumana okukhethekile. Bayakujabulela ukutholakala ukuhlolwa.

Abahambi bakhetha ukungabi bumi futhi isipiliyonivalue ngendlela yalo eguquguquka kakhulu, babheka izindlela, futhi bakha ukuxhumana okubanzi. Bayakujabulela ukutholakala ukuhlola.
