Izinjini zokusesha

Amakhasi e-AMP, amakhasi e-turbo, ukukhomba... okuhle, okubi...

Kufanele uhambe ngokushesha!

Sekuyisikhathi eside i-google icindezela amakhasi e-AMP. Ngoba idatha isho lokho izikhathi zokulayisha ikhasi kuthinta ulwazi lomsebenzisi...ngokusobala. Idlule kwezinye amabala aqinile kodwa i iphrojekthi yomthombo ovulekile iyaqhubeka. Kodwa ngesikhathi sokubhala kusahlolwa.

Ungaziphindi

I-AMP ichaza kabusha inqwaba yomaka be-HTML, futhi ikhawulela iziphi izibaluli ongazisebenzisa. Manje ake ucabange, unekhasi lewebhu, futhi ufuna ukunikeza inguqulo ye-AMP. Lokhu akuyona into engenziwa ngokohlelo , ngakho-ke kuzodingeka udlule kuwo wonke amakhasi, uthole ukuthi imininingwane ye-AMP isekela ini futhi ulungise ikhasi ngokufanele...ubunzima obukhulu, lokhu uma ufuna ukunikeza ulwazi olungaguquki phakathi kwekhasi elizenzakalelayo nekhasi le-AMP.

Nganquma ukungakwenzi. Ngakho-ke ngiphatha amakhasi e-AMP njengombhalo kanye ne-CSS. Konke okunye ngiyakuhlubula. Lokhu ngokuqinisekile akukhona lokho ama-devs e-AMP abekucabanga ngomgomo wokuthuthukisa ulwazi lomsebenzisi...Kodwa okungenani ngingakwazi ukuwenza ngokuzenzakalelayo.

Ngokufanelekile ungafuna ukushicilela iwebhusayithi/amawebhusayithi akho nge-AMP engqondweni kusukela ekuqaleni, ukuze ugcine izinto zingaguquki. Kodwa uma ubhalela i-AMP, okwakho kuphela ukubhala i-AMP, engeyinhle njengoba izwakala njengengadi ebiyelwe ngodonga. Ukubhekana ne-HTML kanye ampHTML i-churn eminingi ukuyigcina. Enye i-AMP izingxenye zewebhu selwenzile emuva W3 izindinganiso. Kodwa iningi lakhona namanje okokuhlola futhi ngubani owaziyo ukuthi i-AMP izoba yizinga noma into ye-google nje. Ngenothi elihle ibhayidu kubonakala sengathi iyayisekela (kanye nayo I-MIPamakhasi...yize engabonakali egcinwe kahle...).

Wonke lawa mafayela a okulondoloziwe kumaseva enjini yokusesha. Lokhu nakho akubukeki kulungile kimi. Iphrojekthi yomthombo ovulekile we-AMP kufanele okungenani inikeze a uyakwazi ukuzibambaiseva yamakhasi ewebhu, noma ngabe ngokwezinjongo zokuthuthukisa.

Yandex

Yandex futhi inamakhasi ayo e-spin...turbo, aqine nakakhulu, njengoba awukwazi ukufaka i-CSS yangokwezifiso ekhasini, kodwa kufanele ukusethe ukusuka kudeshibhodi yabaphathi bewebhu ye-Yandex. Futhi amakhasi e-turbo awawona amakhasi. Uhlinzeka ngokuphakelayo kwe-RSS, noma ifayela elikhulu le-YAML elinawo wonke amakhasi ofuna ukuwanikeza kuwo i-turbo ifomu. Futhi i-Yandex iyayihlikihla bese iyisebenzisela emakhasini abo okusesha...kakhulu ngewebhu ecacayo...

Uyini umgomo?

Amakhasi asheshayo abukeka emuhle? I-ADS elayisha ngokushesha? Ulandelela emthonjeni? Iphethini kimi ibukeka njengokuhlanganiswa okuqondile kwazo zonke izingxenye ezisuka kumsebenzisi, ikhasi lokusesha, kanye nokuqukethwe, ukuya ngqo enjinini yokusesha. Isiqondiso siwukwakha kabanzi eduze kwezingadi ezinezindonga ezilungiselelwe kahle ( uyaqhaqhazela).

Ngethemba ukuthi nginephutha. Ngingathanda ukubona i-amp incibilika ibe yini i-HTML6 noma into efana naleyo, ngokuqinisekile angizamukeli izingxenye zokuhlola. Iningi lewebhusayithi lipheqululwa kumakhalekhukhwini. Ukwenza umehluko phakathi "kwedeskithophu" kanye "neselula" ngesikhathi sejubane kungaba yingozi uma namuhla abantu bethuthukisa amafoni abo ngokushesha kunamakhompuyutha abo edeskithophu namakhompyutha aphathekayo, ukuze ifoni yabo igcine isiyidivayisi yabo eshesha kakhulu. Amakhasi kufanele aklanyelwe ukusebenziseka ngaso sonke isikhathi, indlela "esabelayo", engathwalisa kanzima ukuthuthukiswa kwewebhusayithi, isewukuhweba okungcono kunamakhasi ewebhu anezinguqulo eziningi ezinesiphithiphithi.

Kukhona futhi gemini

Uma ufuna amakhasi ewebhu angasindi, i gemini I-protocol ingasebenza lezo, ingabe iziphequluli ezinkulu zizoyisekela? Mhlawumbe akunjalo...njengoba ingakwazi ukubonisa izikhangiso..
