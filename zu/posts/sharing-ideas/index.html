ukwabelana ngemibono

Kuthiwani uma bekunendlela yokunikeza imilenze emibonweni?

Kukhona isisho esithi imibono emihle inemilenze yayo. Angicabangi ukuthi iqiniso lelo. Impumelelo yombono incike entweni esondelene kakhulu nemakethe. Nganoma yisiphi isikhathi kunabantu abazimisele ukuthenga emibonweni emisha, futhi njenganoma imuphi imakethe, kunesidingo nokuhlinzekwa. Lapho isidingo sincane, umqondo udinga ukufanele Okuningi kunabanye.

Ngamanye amagama, wonke umqondo unemilenze, kepha eminye imilenze imifushane kuneminye. Yini okudingwa yimishini? Izinto ezifanayo eziqhuba ezinye izimakethe. Ipolitiki, iminyaka, izinkathi zonyaka ...

Ingabe wonke umqondo kufanele uwuphishekele? Kunzima ukukala uma umbono ubalulekile noma cha, kepha abanye abantu bangcono ekuboneni imibono emihle, abanye bangcono ukufaka isicelo imibono emihle. Lokhu kusho ukuthi i uqobo imakethe yemibono, esazobekwa emzimbeni, ingaba nengqondo, ngoba yilokho izimakethe okuhle kukho, ukuthola malini okuthile kuwufanele.

Faka i-crypto: Imibono yamathokheni

Ukwenza ithokheni umqondo kusho ukuthi, onakho uhlobo oluthile lwelungeloemcabangweni uqobo. Lokhu kuqala ukubukeka kakhulu amalungelo obunikazi ku-blockchain . Kepha amalungelo obunikazi akuyona into engiyithandayo, nokho ukutholwa kwentengo okukhuthazayo kwemibono okusazosetshenziswa nakanjani (umbono!) Engicabanga ukuthi kufanele ukuwusebenzisa.

Ungakugwema kanjani ukunyathelwa kwelungelo lobunikazi ? Ukunyathelwa kwelungelo lobunikazi kungumphumela we okuxekethile imithetho esetshenziswa kumalungelo obunikazi, ithemba ukuthi njengoba i-crypto space ethos, kufanele landela umthetho olandelwayo , incazelo yamalungelo obunikazi izolandela umbuso oqinile kakhulu owehlisa izindawo ezimpunga.

Ukwenza umbono futhi kusho ukwehlukanisa umqondo ube yizinto eziningi amasheya ubathengise emakethe. Kwenzekani lapho umbono ubanjwe amaqembu amaningi? Amathuba okunyathelwa kwelungelo lobunikazi ayehla ngoba wonke umnikazi wamasheya kuzodingeka ukuthi kungathembeki futhi kuzimisele ukulandela ukuthotshwa kwamalungelo obunikazi, izindleko zokuxhumanisa zilungile uma kuqhathaniswa nokunyathelwa kwamalungelo obunikazi!

Ukugijima kwe-mill crypto economics

Ngingacabanga yonke imibono, njengesivumelwano esihlakaniphile esakha ithokheni. Wonke umuntu ophethe amathokheni unevoti lokwabelana lokuqalisa i- isivuno isicelo ngokumelene nenye inkontileka ehlakaniphile okuthiwa isebenzisa impahla yengqondo ebanjwe "ithokheni yombono". Izicelo ezinjalo zizosulwa ngabantu besithathu, njengama-kleros. Lesi yisikhuthazo, okungukuthi, amalungelo okuvota okuqinisa ukuhoxa, nokwengeza isivuno esinjalo uma samukelwa umuntu wesithathu.
