Amanothi ambalwa ku- (hashcash etholakele) ubufakazi bomsebenzi

ukuqoqwa okufushane kwezigaba zokukhiqiza ubufakazi bokusebenza

Ubufakazi bomsebenzi obuqale ngokomlando ngokusetshenziswa kwe-CPU, bese kuba yi-GPU bese kuthi ngokuya ngokusebenza kahle kwe-ASICS kubongwe ukuncipha nokwenza kahle, ngaphandle kwalapho i-algorithm yakhelwe ukuba ibe ngandlela thile ukumelana okusho ukuthi ingazama ukusuthisa okuthile okusebenza njengebhodlela lokuvimbela ukuzuza ekuqhubekekisweni okuqhubekayo.

Eminye imibono ephawuleka yezinkinga nezicelo:

  • Imemori inzima futhi ibophekile : ubunzima bulula kepha i-tech progession ikwenza kube inketho embi yesikhathi eside, kanti ukuboshwa yi-latency kusho ukuthi une ngokomzimba umkhawulo ekuthuthukisweni okungenzeka, kepha kunzima ukuklama futhi kuxhumene nobunzima be-algorithm. [Izikimu zokukhumbula kanzima] njenge-Yescrypt, i-Argon2, iLyra2 zisetshenziselwe ubufakazi bokusebenza, i-dagger-hashimoto ne-ethhash futhi inkumbulo inzima. Azikho izinhlelo zangempela endle zama-algorithm aboshwe ngememori, ngaphandle kobufakazi besitoreji, ngaphandle kokuthi kulokhu, ukubheka yi okubhekiswe kukho hhayi nje impahla esebenzisekayo ye-hashing alghorithm, ngoba ubufakazi kuleli cala bususelwa kuzinqubo eziyinselele / zokuphendula (..njengoba kufanele ukhombise ukuthi ugcina okuthile hhayi ukuthi uzenze nje ukukugcina ..)

  • Umkhawulokudonsa uboshiwe: ukuhambisa idatha ezindaweni zonke kunqunyelwe kumthamo wesiteshi esisetshenzisiwe. Ukufaka i-bandwidth eboshwe nge-algorithm kusho ukusakazwa kwedatha nokusebenzisa isihlungi (esilula!) Phezu kwaso ukuthola amaphethini awusizo, amandla we-algorithm ancike ekuhlulekeni komhlaseli ukubikezela izinkomba lapho idatha efiselekayo ingahle itholakale khona. Ukuphela kokuqalisa engikwaziyo kutholakala ku- iqhwaqhwa lapho a ngokuqhubekayo i-database enkulu eyenziwe nge i-entropy eningi isetshenziswa njengesisekelo. Ububi bokuboshwa komkhawulokudonsa ukuthi bunobungane ku- ukukala , lelo likhono lokufaka kanye nokufaka inqwaba yezingxenyekazi zekhompiyutha ukufeza amakhono ezingeni lezimboni.

  • Ubunzima buboshiwe : le ncazelo ye-wavy yesandla kakhulu ukusho ukuthi kunzima ukwengeza ngokumelene nento evela okungahleliwe ... okungenani ngaphezulu. Uma unomqondo othile okhiqiza umphumela owufunayo, futhi uzama ukwengeza ukucabanga okunjalo, ungakwenza lokho kuphela uma ungabikezela ukusebenza komqondo ngokwesibonelo amanani enqolobane, noma udale amapayipi asheshayo aziwa njenge-recroutines; lokhu kusho ukuthi amandla we-algorithm ancike namandla ayo mbumbulu ukungahleliwe. Njengesibonelo umqondo we izinkontileka ezihlakaniphile ezimayini , noma enye i-algorithm elanda idatha kusuka ku-blockchain. [RandomX] njengoba isetshenziswe ekusetshenzisweni kwe-monero njengembewu i-hash yama- ~ 50 block ngemuva, isandulela se-randomX singatholakala ku i-cryptonight-adaptive noma okungahleliwe. Ukungahleliwe kuyingxenye eyodwa kuphela yokusetshenziswa, enye ukwenza ukusatshalaliswa kwamathuba okufanisa amandla (isb. Imiyalo esethiwe) yomshini oqondisiwe oyifunayo, esimweni se-randomx ukwakhiwa okuvamile kwe-CPU, inkqubela iyi-(nvidia?: P) i-GPU, esimweni sezinkontileka ezihlakaniphile zokuboniswa kwezibalo zezimayini akufunwa ngoba ukwenziwa kwenqubo eyisisekelo kuconsisa insiza.

  • Ukubulawa kuboshiwe : noma isikhathi ukuboshwa, ngokuvamile kusho ukuthi kudinga imininingwane ethile ukukhiqiza imininingwane ethile, ngokulandelana kwayo (njengokuphinda into efanayo izikhathi eziningi) okudala yazo zonke izinto i-crypto futhi isetshenziswa kakhulu (konke?) Ama-cipher ngoba kuyindlela eqonde kakhulu (futhi esobala) yokuhweba ukubalwa kwe-obfuscation. Lapho usebenzisa lo mqondo ezingeni eliphansi (ukudala umsebenzi we-hashing ongenakulinganiswa), i-algorithm kufanele ibe lula ngangokunokwenzeka, njenge mfushane ukusebenza kwezibalo, njengokuthi kungenzeka ukuhlukanisa umsebenzi ngokusebenzisa izinqubo ezifanayo kuyancishiswa. Ama- [VDF] azuza lokhu, noma, ngaphandle kwezidingo zokusetha, izinhlelo ezinje isinqumookusho ukuthi "okushesha kakhulu kunqoba njalo", yingakho i-VDF ingathathwa njenge-POW kuphela uma ukwenziwa kwaziwa (kufakazelwa?) ukuthi kunganciphisi ngaphezu kokulibaziseka kokuxhumana kwe-Δ (delta) kohlelo okuhlose yisebenzise (isib. inethiwekhi ye-p2p). Noma iyiphi i-algorithm ebhekise ukubopha ukwenziwa ngaphandle kwe-trapdoor isho ukubalwa okungu-1: 1 phakathi kwe-prover ne-verifier, kepha akuyona i-deterministic, okuyisidingo (ngiyavuma ukusho ..) se-POW. [I-Unprll] kwakuyimali esetshenziswa ngokulandelana, ukuqinisekiswa kwe-hashes kwakuyingxenye (1/8?), Ukuthuthukisa isilinganiso sokuqinisekisa / se-prover ngenhla kwe-1. Olunye uhlamvu lwemali olubonakala lusebenzisa i-hashing elandelanayo pascalcoin ngokuxuba kobunzima bememori.
