ukubhala igobolondo

Imicabango yami nge-shell script

IBash/ zshukubhala kweShell kwenza umsebenzi wenziwe. Kubuye kubi impela. Mhlawumbe kuhloswe. Okuncane ofuna ukukubuka kuzoba kufushane imibhalo ezoba yikho. Angiyena umlandeli wokusebenzisa i-bash / zsh njengegobolondo elisebenzisanayo, inani lama-plugins adingekayo ukwenza okuhlangenwe nakho okuhle liyasabeka, futhi yonke i-plugin eyengeziwe iyisihibe sokungenzeka kwe-shell slow downs. Ungalokothi-unciphise inani lomsebenzi owenziwe yi-psprint nge zinit nabangane kuyamangalisa kakhulu (futhi futhi uhlobo lokukhetha okuwukuphela komphathi we-plugin uma ufuna ukusebenzisa i-zsh njengegobolondo elixhumanayo). Kodwa-ke akulixazululi iqiniso lokuthi wonke lawo ma-plugins alayishiwe angama- okungabelwanga ngakho inkumbulo phakathi (mhlawumbe) kwezinqubo eziningi zeShell.

Igobolondo lezinhlanziyigobolondo lami engilithandayo ngesikhathi sokubhala. Inani lama-plugins afakiwe lincane kakhulu, ngoba lenza izinto eziningi ongazifuna kuShell ngaphandle kwebhokisi . Iskripthi sihamba kancane kune-zsh kepha akekho onendaba nesivinini sokubhala ngakho-ke kulungile. I-syntax yinhle ngefa le-ML (njengoJulia) ngezitatimende zokuphela, esikhundleni sezinsimbi ezisontekile. Ukubhala imibhalo kusetshenziswa kakhulu kune-bash / zsh kepha futhi kufundeka kakhulu. Izinto ezinjengokufaka esikhundleni senqubo, ukuhlanganiswa kwentambo kanye namapayipi kugcina isenzo esithe xaxa. Ine global okuguqukayo, okwabiwe kuzo zonke izinqubo ezisebenzayo, kanye indawo yonke okuguqukayo, okwabiwe ngezikhathi zonke, okwenza izinto zibe lula ngaphandle kokubhala konke phansi nokuthola imibhalo ekhohlwayo.

Elvish ingabe igobolondo elinokusekelwa kwezinto eziningi, njenge-daemon noma imodi yewebhu evumela ukuthi ithumele imiyalo kude ...? Ngicabanga ukuthi kuningi kakhulu futhi ngiqala ukuyibiza ngokuqumba. I-IMHO igobolondo kufanele ilwele ukuba lula, futhi ibe nokuqukethwe. Ngicabanga ukuthi amagobolondo kufanele aklanyelwe ukuphatha izimo lapho izinqubo ziveza inani elivelele lezikhathi, eziziphatha ngokungaguquki kusukela ekuqaleni kuze kube sekugcineni futhi eziqukethe ngokwazo ukuze zikwazi ukusingatha izingozi ngomusa. Ngakho-ke ngaphandle kwazo zonke ezinye izici (njengedatha ehlelekile) ... amademoni awangisizi.

Ion Igobolondo kuyinto cishe posix Shell kusuka ku-redox, nayo esebenza ku-unix, ngenhloso ye- futhiukusebenza emafasiteleni, noma kungasebenzi ATTOW. Inhloso yalo ukumane ube yigobolondo elingcono, ngakho-ke kuthatha okuhle kusuka ku-posix bese kungezwa kukho. Kubukeka sengathi kugxile ekunikezeni ulimi oluhle lokubhala, kepha hhayi ulwazi oluhle kakhulu lokusebenzisana, igobolondo lezinhlanzi lingonakalisile, futhi angibuyeli kugobolondo ngaphandle isigaba sokuqala ukuxhumana.

Igobolondo lamafutha inomgomo wokufaka esikhundleni se-bash, okuyinto encomekayo, noma kunjalo ibhekise kuphela ezinhlelweni ze-unix ezingenayo iWindows ebonakalayo. Igxile ekulungiseni ungqimba lokuhambisana kwe-bash kuze kube manje, ngezinhlelo zesikhathi esizayo zokugxila engxenyeni yokuxhumana. Uma sibheka iqiniso lokuthi ingxenye exhumanayo yegobolondo ayikabhalwa, kungenza ngibe nokungabaza okuncane ngekusasa layo futhi yize ngithemba ukuthi ayibhaliwe, I feat ingase ivule umusi ngaphambi kokuba ifinyelele ezinhlosweni zayo ebezihlosiwe. Futhi ukuntuleka kokusekelwa kwamawindows akulungile.

I-Rash igobolondo elinokusekelwa kwedatha ehlelekile esebenza nayo Racket izinto, lapho ngibheka i-racket ngaphezulu ngizozama, yize isipiliyoni sami esedlule nge igobolondo le-emacs ] ayiyinhle ngempela, ngoba eshell isebenza kuphela ngaphakathi kwama-emac, futhi ayisebenzi kahle njenge igobolondo lesistimu ... ngaphandle kokuthi uhlelo lwakho lube ngama-emacs (yize ama-emacs engumhleli wami omkhulu futhi ngiyabhala ezinye lisp, akuyona uhlelo lwami lokusebenza). (Lapha kufanele futhi ngikhulume ngeJulia REPL, impendulo engcono kakhulu yanoma yiluphi ulimi lohlelo).

Igobolondo lamandla I-Powershell, igobolondo elikhulu leWindows lisebenza futhi ku-linux nakuma-macs, ngenxa yesikhulumi esiphambanweni NET . Kepha kunesenzo kakhulu, kuhamba kancane, futhi mhlawumbe (akuqinisekisiwe) kugcwele amafasitela ; Kuyinto enkulu kimi.
