izinhlobo ze-3D platformers

hlobo luni lwemidlalo olungabizwa ngokuthi ama-platformer?

Iplatifomu ye-3D uhlobo lomdlalo lapho unekhamera futhi inhloso enkulu ukusuka endaweni u-A kuye ku-B.

Izici ezivamile

  • izindawo zokuhlola : wonke amazinga agcwele izindawo zokuhlola, ukwenza ipulatifomu kuyindaba yokwenza ukulandelana kwezenzo (okokufaka), amaphutha ajwayelekile, futhi umphumela uvame umdlalo uphelile . Izindawo zokuhlola zinciphisa iphuzu lobuhlungu elivame kakhulu lama-platformers ... kusukela ekuqaleni.

  • ijubaneama-platformers yimidlalo ejwayelekile kakhulu ukusheshisa ukugijima, ngoba adinga ukucaciswa kuzona zonke izinyathelo, ayafana nokudlala insimbi yomculo, lapho uma wehluleka ukwenza inhlanganisela efanele, umphumela -ngathandeki.

  • amamephu nezengezo : kunezinhlobonhlobo zamamephu njengoba amamephu kuyiyona eyinhloko okuqukethwe yomdlalo, imiphakathi nayo iyakhuthazwa ukwakha nokuhlola izakhiwo ezahlukahlukene zamamephu nezinhlobo zezithiyo ezingaphakathi kwayo.

  • abadlali abaningi nokuncintisana : ukuncintisana kukhona kokubili ngendlela ye- wabaphambili ne-PvP isitayela somjaho ukufana. Kodwa-ke, ngenxa yophahla lwamakhono aphakeme, kanye nengozi ejwayelekile yokwehluleka ngaphakathi kokubulawa, amabhodi wabaphambili ayindlela eyinhloko yokuncintisana.

Izigaba ezingaphansi

Ama-Platformers angahlukaniswa ngokuya ngohlobo lwe-physics abayisebenzisayo ukukhipha ukunyakaza:

  • Ama-Surfers : ukundiza, ukukhukhuleka, imigwaqo kanye nemithambeka kulawula ukuqondiswa kwe-PG, okuvame ukuhamba njalo kusukela ekuqaleni kuze kube sekugcineni, kuhamba ngokushesha okukhulu.

  • Ababambeki : sebenzisa ama-projectiles, umfutho we-angular nokukhuphuka ukuze uhambise amapulatifomu, amamephu avame ukwenziwa ngezinyathelo ezibekiwe zokuhlola, lapho zonke izinyathelo ukulandelana kwezenzo; ngenxa yalokhu, ukuhamba kwe-PG kuhamba kancane kunokuphazamiseka okuvamile. Kuvamile ukuthola ama-puzzle ngaphakathi kwama-grapplers.

  • UParkour : ukugxuma kabili, ukuxhuma i-bunny, ukulawula umoya, i-rocket-jumping amakhono ajwayelekile anikezwa ama-PG kulolu hlobo lwama-platformer. Kuvame ukuhlanganiswa I-FPS izakhi ezinezakhi zepaki ngenhloso yokuxhaphaza izikhali ukunqoba izithiyo.
