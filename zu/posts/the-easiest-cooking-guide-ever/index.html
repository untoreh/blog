Umhlahlandlela wokupheka olula kunayo yonke

I-bang engcono kakhulu yomhlahlandlela wesikhathi sokufunda sokupheka

Ake sihlukanise ukupheka ngezinyathelo ezine bese sihlaziya ngasinye sazo:

  • Ukuhlanza

  • Ukuxuba

  • Ukushisa

  • Ukuphumula

Ukuhlanza

Ukuthenga

Esinyathelweni sokuqala sibheka nenqubo ye- ukuzuza izithako. Izitolo zesaphulelo zimnandi ngenxa yeminikelo ebanzi, ungasebenza ezungeze impilo eshalofini emfushane iqhwa noma yini ongahlose ukuyisebenzisa ngokushesha.

Ukugcina nokulungiselela

Ake sicabangele amanye amaqembu anezeluleko ezithile:

  • Izithelo nemifino: Ukufaka emanzini / uviniga okungenani isigamu sehora kungasiza inqubo yokuhlanza, uma zigcwele ukungcola. Kungasiza futhi ukuvuselelwa kabusha, yize kunjalo kuleli cala sikhuluma okungenani isigamu sosuku lokujuluka (futhi kungabi ngaphezu kwamahora angama-48, ngoba amabhaktheriya). Ngemifino ungasebenzisa cishe yonke into, kepha imvamisa siyazilahla izingxenye ezinzima ezithatha isikhathi eside ukupheka, kepha uma uhlukanisa izingxenye ezinzima, unga hlanganisa uzipheke ngokwehlukana (ukwenza, isobho noma izinongo, noma okhilimu). Izithelo zezithelo zinjalo futhi, njengomthetho-wesithupha, kufanele kuphontshwe amakhasi anezikali, lapho ubushelelezi besikhumba kungenzeka kakhulu (buhlanzeke) buhlanzeke futhi budliwe, ngaphandle kobhanana. Ungayisebenzisi ikhasi lezithelo ezisawolintshi ngaphandle kokuthi kuthiwe kungokwemvelo. Akuwona wonke amakhasi ezithelo amnandi, amanye anobuthi ngokwemvelo, njengophayinaphu, noma ungaphikisana nokuthi amanani abalulekile. ( i.e. kukhona utshwala obenziwe ngembewu ye-apula, ngaphandle kwembewu yanoma yisiphi isithelo esivela ku- rosacea umndeni wezitshalo oqukethe umthamo omncane we-cyanide).

  • Imidumba: Ama-legumes omisiwe adinga ukuwashwa okumbalwa, ngoba angakwazi ukuqoqa uthuli nezinye izinhlayiya ngesikhathi sokulondolozwa. Nakekela izindawo zokugcina, izinhlobo eziningi zezimbungulu zingondla kuzo, zisebenzise eqinile amabhokisi okugcina, hhayi izikhwama. Inani lokuwasha lincike ekutheni kanjani ukujiya igobolondo le legume. Imidumba ehlukaniswe njengobhontshisi nama-chickpeas idinga amahora amahle angama-24, imidumba emincane efana nodali ilungile ngamahora ambalwa.

  • Izimpushana: Njengofulawa noshukela. Uma indawo yokugcina ingomile ngokwanele, ungahle udinge hlunga impushana yokudiliza iqoqo elisungulwe ngenxa ye- umswakama . Izimbungulu ezinjengezinundu nazo azilungile kufulawa, zisebenzisa isitoreji esihle.

Ukuzisika

Ungasika ngemimese, kepha kunamathuluzi amaningi ashibhile ongawathenga asiza ukunciphisa imifino nezithelo zibe izingcezu, noma ukuxova izimpushana ezimahhadla noma ezicolekileyo. Kungenzeka basebenzise indlela ethile yasentwasahlobo, noma babe nesimo esilingana nezithelo / imifino ethile.

Amaqanda

Amaqanda anokutholwa okubanzi ekuphekeni, asetshenziswa njenge:

  • ama-emulsifiers (gcina izinto zihlangene ngendlela ewuketshezi ebushelelezi)

  • izibopho (hlala izinto ndawonye ngendlela eqinile)

  • imvubelo (ingabamba umoya futhi ivuse inhlama) Amaqanda enziwa ngesikhupha (amafutha) nomhlophe (amaprotheni namanzi). Umhlophe usebenziseka kakhulu ekubambeni / ekubiliseni okuqinile, isikhupha se-emulsifying cream, noma ekuhlobiseni. Ukukhumbula la maqiniso, khetha ukuthi uwasebenzisa kanjani amaqanda, bese usebenzisa i isihlukanisi seqanda ukusebenzisa i-yolk nabamhlophe njengezithako ezahlukahlukene. Lapho ushaya amaqanda, uwafudumeze (angadluli ku-30C) futhi engeza amaconsi ambalwa kalamula (noma i-citric acid) kungasiza inqubo yokufaka.

Ukuxuba

Ngesikhathi sokuxuba sizama ukunikeza ilungelo isimo futhi ukungaguquguquki esihlokweni sethu.

Ukuhleleka

Imvamisa ufuna ukuxuba ngamaqembu, okuqinile nokuqinile, noketshezi okunoketshezi. Ngama-solid siqonde impuphu njengezithako, nangoketshezi noma yini engabambi ukuma kwayo. Ngaphandle kokuthi ufuna umoya, lapho-ke uhlanganisa ushukela nebhotela.

Kuyabanda noma Kuyashisa?

Uma wenza ikhekhe ufuna ibhotela lincibilike kuqala ngaphambi ukuxubana, uma wenza u-ayisikhilimu, ufuna izingcezu ezingcezwini zeqhwa lokho Kuzohlanganiswa ndawonye. Uma uhlanganisa ama-alimondi noshukela ukwenza i-nougat, ufuna ukuthi babande kuwo gwema i-caramelization kashukela, obekungavimba ukwakheka kokunamathisela.

Isikhathi

Kwesinye isikhathi kubalulekile ukunaka isikhathi senqubo yokuxuba. Ukwenza inhlama yamathets ufuna ukuthi ibe mfushane, ukuze ukuvimbela ukuthuthukiswa kwezibopho ze-gluten, obekunganikeza ukuqina kwekhekhe elibhakwe. Ukuxuba uketshezi oluningi osuvele uphethe umoya (njengamaqanda ashayiwe noma ibhotela / ushukela ukuxuba) kwephula amagwebu omoya futhi kuvumela wonke umoya phunyuka umshayi. Ngokuphambene nalokho lapho uxuba inhlama yesinkwa, ufuna ukunika inqubo yokuxuba isikhathi esanele sokuvumela izibopho ze-gluten ukuthi zizinikele ukuqina enhlama ebalulekile ngemuva kwalokho ukubumba isinkwa bese usinika amandla wokusebenza vuka ngesikhathi senqubo yokusekela.

Ubuningi

Ukungeza wonke amafutha ngasikhathi sinye, njengamafutha, kungenza inhlama okuxekethile ukungaguquguquki kwayo, kuyaphula kube yizicucu ezincane. Ufuna ukufaka kancane kancane amafutha ku ibhalansi ukudalwa kwe-gluten nge ukumuncwa kwamafutha . Ngendlela efanayo engeza izithako ezinjengamanzi noma ubisi ngasikhathi sinye akuwona umqondo omuhle. Kulula ukusebenza ngenhlama lapho kuhlanganiswa khona okuqinile neziphuzo, futhi kuyasiza amaphutha amabala phakathi imithamo ngokushesha kunokuba kamuva. Izithako ezahlukahlukene zinokuqukethwe okuhlukile kwamanzi. Ungathola ukuthi isithako sinamanzi angakanani ngokuhlola amaqiniso wokudla okunempilo bese ukukhipha kusuka kunani eliphelele (imvamisa100g ), i- Isamba samaprotheni, amafutha, nama-carbohydrate. Isisindo samaminerali namavithamini asinakwa. Okuqukethwe okuphelele kwamanzi kubalulekile ukugwema inhlama ekubeni yi-batter ne-viceversa. Ezinye izithako (njengamazambane, nezithelo / imifino ehlanganisiwe ngokujwayelekile) zingabamba amanzi amaningi, ngoba imicu ivimbela ezinye izithako ezifana nofulawa ukuthi zingamunci amanzi amaningi, kepha inhlama evelayo isagcina inamathele futhi kunzima ukuyiphatha.

Imvubelo

  • Imvubelo yamakhemikhali: i-sodium / potassium bicarbonate noma iphawuda wokubhaka , engezwe ku ukuphela wenqubo yokuxuba, khetha ukuxubana okusheshayo.

  • Imvubelo engokwemvelo: imvubelo kusuka kubhiya, noma iyogathi, noma kwifomu elomile (lempuphu). Ukungezwa kancane kancane ngesikhathi senqubo yokuxuba.

    • Ama-ejenti afaka imvubelo asebenza kangcono ngamazinga okushisa ngaphezulu kune20C futhi Ngaphansi kune30C.

    • Ukuvutshelwa kuyaqhubeka inqobo nje uma amazinga okushisa engaphezulu kuka-0, kwehlisa ijubane nayo amazinga okushisa aphansi futhi futhi amazinga okushisa aphezulu . Sebenzisa ifriji ne-ovini (ukukhanya) ukuya lawula singakanani isikhathi sokunikelwa ekuvutsheni.

    • Umswakama ophezulu kubalulekile ekuthuthukiseni imvubelo. Ingilazi yamanzi eduze kwenhlama ingasiza (futhi ibagcine endaweni ebiyelwe ukuze umswakama wesicupho , noma umboze ngendwangu)

    • ushukela omncane owengeziwe kungasiza inqubo yokuvutshelwa, kepha imvamisa okusanhlamvu okuvela enhlama sekuvele kushukela okwanele

    • Usawoti can yehlisa kancane ijubane ukuvutshelwa, nosawoti omningi ungabulala imvubelo ngokuphelele.

    • Inqubo yokuvutshelwa iguqula ushukela ube amaprotheni, isikhutha kanye nama-asidi. Ukuvutshelwa okuningi nenhlama izokonakala. Njengomthetho wesithupha,2 days ngenhlama egcinwe ku lokushisa ekamelweni, 2 weeks ngenhlama egcinwe ku- ifriji.

  • Imvubelo yemishini: amaqanda ashayiwe, ibhotela enokhilimu, izithelo ezihlanganisiwe . Lezi zithako ziyakwazi umoya ogibe ngakho-ke yenza ukwanda lapho uxubene ngaphakathi kwenhlama ngesikhathi sokubhaka njengoba umoya uzama phunyuka kusukela kumabhamuza.

  • Imvubelo kamama iyinhlama ebilisiwe ngokulingene, igcinwa njalo ihlanzekile (ngokufaka amanzi nofulawa ngezikhathi ezithile, nokususa indawo endala), ejwayele ukwenziwa ukukhahlela phansi ukuvutshelwa kwezinhlama ezintsha, isilinganiso saso singu-1 / 4..1 / 3 wenhlama entsha. Izindawo ezinkulu ezimnyama zimbi (kusho ukonakala), amachashazi amancane kakhulu alungile, imvamisa umphumela we-oxidation.

  • Umkhiqizo ovutshiwe:

    • Iwayini: ukuvutshelwa kwe- izithelo (yize ngokusemthethweni ukuvutshelwa kwezithelo kusuka vitis vinifera kungabizwa ngewayini, ngicabanga ukuthi kulungile ukwenza ukuvutshelwa kwezithelo njengewayini.)[1]

    • Ubhiya: ukuvutshelwa kwe okusanhlamvu

    • I-yogurt: ukuvutshelwa kwe- ubisi

    • Ushizi (Hhayi konke): Ukuvutshelwa kwe- ubisi olujiyile ngemuva kokuhlunga

    • Abanye:

      • IKombucha: Ibilile itiye

      • Mead: kubilile uju

    • Uviniga: kubilile iwayini (i-yeast yeast iguqula ushukela ube yi-alcohol, uviniga yeasts uguqula utshwala bube yi-acetic acid) Kunezinhlanganisela zamakhemikhali ezingangezwa kuziphuzo ezivutshiwe ukulawula i-asidi, umbala, ukunambitheka ..:

  • i-ascorbic acid (uvithamini C) (ivimbela namachibi)

  • i-potassium bicarbonate (inciphisa i-asidi)

  • i-anhydride enesibabule (ibamba kancane ukuvutshelwa)

  • i-tartaric acid (ivimbela crystallization ewayinini)

  • I-Bentonite (ivimbela amafu)

  • I-Arabic Gum (iqinisa ukunambitheka)

  • Umhlaba we-diatomaceous (kubi, ungasebenzisi)

  • i-potassium metabisolphite (ingagwenywa) Uhlu aluphelele, njalo cwaninga noma yiliphi ikhemikhali olifunayo ofuna ukulisebenzisa emikhiqizweni yakho ebilile.

Izilinganiso zama-Doughs

Ama-preps ahlukile adinga izilinganiso ezahlukahlukene zalezi zithako:

  • Ufulawa (okusanhlamvu, amantongomane, imbewu, imidumba, njll.)

  • Amaqanda (izithelo noma iyogathi, njll.)

  • Amanzi (Ubisi, amasoso, iwayini elihlanjululwe, njll.)

  • Ushukela (okucacile, okuluhlaza, ukhukhunathi, njll.)

  • Amafutha (Amafutha, ibhotela, isinqumelo, njll.) Ungathola ama-cheat sheet kuwebhu afinyelela kokuhle itafula izilinganiso zezinhlobo zebhikawozi ezivame kakhulu. Akuyona yonke into eshintshanayo; ufulawa ongekho okusanhlamvu awunayo i-gluten eyanele futhi inhlama evelayo iyohlala iqhubeka ngokuxakaniseka, idinga ama-ejenti amaningi abophayo. Kunezinye izindlela zoshukela ezifana lezwasil, erythritol , njll. kepha ushukela nawo ubalulekile ekwakhiweni kwawo kwamakristalu, okwengeza ukuthungwa, futhi okusiza ukwakheka kwamabhamuza omoya. Isakhiwo se-crystalline asihlali siphindaphindeka kwezinye izindlela zikashukela, ngakho-ke kungadingeka ukuthi okungenani ulinganise nama-ejenti amaningi abilayo.

Ukushisa

  • Ukuphendula kukaMaillard : Amaprotheni, amafutha kanye noshukela ahlangana phakathi kwawo abe ama-molecule ayinkimbinkimbi. Yilokhu okunikeza ukunambitheka lapho upheka. Iyanda ngamazinga okushisa aphezulu nokuqukethwe kwamanzi aphansi (kuze kufike ekushiseni), ngakho-ke ivelele kakhulu ku- ubuso wento ephekiwe. Lokhu kulandela ukuthi uma ukusika Izinto zibe yizicucu ezincane, ngakho-ke ukwandisa indawo engaphezulu uzophela ngokunambitheka okwengeziwe (kungahle kube nento enambitheka kakhulu). Kuyafaneleka lapho kubhakwa, ukwenza i-toast, ukosa kanye nokuthosa, zonke izindlela zokupheka ezinamanzi aphansi noma angenawo nhlobo.

  • I-Caramelization : lapho ushukela uxubana namanzi ngethempelesha ephezulu, njengoba ukusabela kuqhubeka, amanzi ayahwamuka futhi ushukela usha ubemuncu. I-Caramelization isiza ekunikezeni ukudla uqweqwe oluqinile, nolushubile.

  • Kuyakhuphuka: Ukufeza ukukhuphuka kuvamile ukushisa kuhhavini ngaphambi kokudala ukuze kwenziwe umehluko ophakeme ngokwanele wokushisa ku lukhuni ingaphandle lenhlama, njengokuthi umusi ubhajwe ngaphakathi kuqinisa inhlama ibheke phezulu. Kepha kufanele uqaphele ngokulinganisela amazinga okushisa nge-prep yonke[2] amazinga omswakama. Uma i-prep isome kakhulu, ukushisa kwangaphambili kuzoholela ku kushe uqweqwe nengaphakathi elingaphekiwe.

  • Iphuzu lokubhema : Amafutha ahlukene shabalala emazingeni okushisa ahlukene, cishe:

    • ibhotela: ~ 180C

    • uwoyela womnqumo: ~ 200C

    • amafutha avela kumantongomane: ~ 210C

    • uwoyela ovela embewini: ~ 220C Ungadluli kulawa mazinga okushisa kuye ngokuthi ngawaphi amafutha owasebenzisayo. Lokhu akunakugwenywa lapho usebenzisa i- izitofu , ezimweni lapho okuqukethwe kwamanzi ekudleni kungalinganisa amazinga okushisa aphezulu kakhulu. ( Lokungeniswa ama-cooktops akuvumela ukuthi ulawule amazinga okushisa). Izinqubo zokuthosa noma uku-browning zingadinga amazinga okushisa aphezulu, kepha ukugcina ngaphansi kwephuzu lokubhema kusenganikeza imiphumela eyamukelekayo noma kunjalo.

  • Ukuphefumula : Ukusebenzisa ifeni lapho ukubhaka kunikeza ukushisa okuthe xaxa ngaphakathi kuhhavini, kepha futhi kuyomisa isivinini sokulungiselela ngokushesha, kungadala ukupheka okungaphekiwe. Musa ukubeka i-prep eduze nefeni uqobo, umfutho womoya mubi.

  • Ukunamathela : Sebenzisa amapani nezinti ezingezona izinti, ngaphandle kwalapho i-prep ingamanzi kakhulu, njengesobho noma imifino ebilisiwe. Ngokubhaka, amashidi angenantambo akuvumela ngezifiso ukwakheka kwenhlama, futhi ngokushiya umngcele wamashidi athile, kuvumela ukukhipha okulula kokulungiselela okubhakiwe kusuka epanini (okungabekwa esitsheni ukuphumula). Womabili amapani non-stick namashidi enziwe teflon , futhi i-teflon ayidlali kahle ngamazinga okushisa ngenhla kuka-280C.

  • Amajamu : Lapho wenza ujamu, ukushisa yiokudala evumela ushukela ukuthi ubophele kuma-pectin molecule (avela emanzini). I-acid ilinganisa lolu shintsho, alwanele futhi kuzothatha nzima ukuthi ushukela uphume emanzini, ngokweqile futhi amaketanga kashukela abamba amanzi azobe evuleke kakhulu, angalokothi avumele i-jelly ukuthi ikwakhe (ikushiye nesiraphu efana noketshezi) . Ungakhuphula i-jellyness ngokungeza ngokwengeziwe i-pectin (esitolo esithengiwe), noma uqaphele ngezithelo, ngoba izithelo ezahlukahlukene zinokuqukethwe okuhlukile kwe-pectin, futhi i-pectin iyancipha njengoba izithelo zivuthwa.

  • Ukunciphisa Amanzi : Uma ubilisa ama-preps njengesobho, ujamu, ama-chutneys, amasoso, okhilimu, njll. Lapho uwagcina esitofini, kulapho amanzi ehwamuka enhlanganisweni ... (kusobala). Inhlanganisela ejiyile izokwenza fafaza ama-bubble aqinile (enza ukungcola ekhishini), ngakho-ke thinta izinga lokushisa lokupheka ngokufanele ngobuningi be-prep, isibindi, inqubo yokubilisa kufanele ibe kancane. Amanzi amancane asho okuningi kugxilwe ama-flavour, hlola njalo imithamo yezinongo, bese uhlela kusengaphambili ukuthi ulindele ukukhipha amanzi angakanani, njengomthetho wesithupha, izinongo zithatha u-1-3% wesisindo esiphelele sezithako eziqinile. Uma sisebenzisa umusi, kusho ukuthi ibhodwe kufanele libe ne- isivalo kuyo (ukungavumeli umphunga uphunyuke), awudingi umpheki wokucindezela, ukupheka ngomphunga, abapheki bokucindezela basheshisa inqubo. Ungakugwema ukubilisa imifino ngokusebenzisa amanzi amancane nje ukukhahlela ukuhwamuka; Imifino eminingi ikhipha ingxenye enhle yokuqukethwe kwamanzi lapho ivezwa amazinga okushisa aphakeme, amanzi akhishwe nawo agcina ehwamuka aqeda inqubo yokupheka imifino (imifinobapheke ngamanzi abo ). Ukupheka izimbobo (ngokwesibonelo namazambane) kusheshisa inqubo yokupheka, njengoba ukushisa kukwazi ukufinyelela ezingxenyeni zangaphakathi ngokushesha. Uma usebenzisa iwayini noma uviniga wezinongo / isobho qaphela ukuthi kokubili utshwala (obuvela ewayinini), kanye ne-acetic acid (evela kuviniga) anobuthi lapho ziphenduka igesi, (zimbi emaphashini akho). Iningi lokuqukethwe kotshwala kuyaphuma ngesikhathi senqubo yokubilisa ( cishe 3/4 ngesigamu sehora). Unganciphisi uviniga kakhulu, njengoba i-acetic acid egxilile ingakwazi konakalisa amapani ensimbi (iTerracotta isingatha kangcono). Ukubilisa iwayini / uviniga kuzonuka kabi nganoma iyiphi indlela, kepha ukunambitheka okungeziwe ku-prep kuzokufanele, (kepha ungapheli ukukwenza). Amanzi nomphunga kunciphisa ukusabela kweMaillard. Ukuphakama kuthinta izinga lokushisa elibilayo ngenxa yengcindezi yasemkhathini, ukuphakama kokuphakama kungaphansi kokushisa okubilayo.

Ukuphumula

Amanye ama-preps kufanele adliwe khona manjalo, njenge ama-souffles abanye bangadinga izikhathi ezimbalwa zokuphumula:

  • Amakhukhi kufanele avunyelwe ukuba aphumule aze afinyelele izinga lokushisa ekamelweni, ukukhipha umhwamuko osalayo. Ungalokothi ubeke amakhukhi asuka kuhhavini ngqo ebhokisini elivaliwe.

  • Amakhekhe esipanji, nokufaka amalungiselelo anzima (njenge tiramisu ) badinga isikhathi sefriji ukumunca izinto zokuhlobisa.

  • Amaphayi adinga isikhathi sokupholisa ukuvumela uqweqwe ukuba luqine; (uma uzama ukusika ucezu ngenkathi insimbi isashisa kuhhavini, izowahlazeka ezandleni zakho)

  • Ama-Ice Creams adinga ifriji ngemuva kwenqubo yokuhlangana, ukubuyisa i-viscosity efanele ngamazinga okushisa aphansi.

  • Amawayini agcinwe eshalofini isikhathi eside angavela (angenabungozi) amakristalu phansi. Iwayini noViniga kukhula izinsalela isikhathi esengeziwe njengoba imvubelo engaphakathi kubo ifa. (Ama-Dregs empeleni enziwe ngama-bacteria afile, kuyadliwa, noma ngabe kubaba).

  • I-Puff pastry isebenzisa imvubelo yemishini kusuka ekuhlukanisweni kwezingqimba ezahlukahlukene zenhlama, futhi idinga ukuphumula ngemuva kokusetshenzwa kwendwangu ngayinye eyengeziwe.

  • Amasaladi adinga ukuphumula esiqandisini, ukuze amunce izinongo, uwoyela neviniga.

  • Okokupheka kwe-pizza, i-focaccia ne-calzone, ukuphumula inhlama (okungenani isigamu sehora) ngemuva kokuyibeka epanini, kuvumela imvubelo eyengeziwe ukwenzeka ngaphambi kokufaka okokufaka.

  • Ama-emulsion kawoyela namanzi awahlali isikhathi eside (ngaphandle kwabasizi abanjengo lecithin ), amasosi okuphumula akhiwe nge-yogurt isikhathi eside kakhulu azonikeza imiphumela engadingeki.

  • Lapho usomisa imifino noma izithelo obala, qaphela izinyoni, kungenjalo sebenzisa umshini wokomisa noma i-ovini ngesikhathi esiphansi.
