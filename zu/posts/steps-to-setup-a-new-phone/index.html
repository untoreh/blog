Izinyathelo zokusetha ifoni entsha

Landela izinyathelo zokususa ifoni yesitoko ye-android

  • Thola ifoni ngokuya ngokuncamelayo, amadili we-amazon warehouse noma i-aliexpress eyenziwe kabusha ine-bang engcono kakhulu ye-buck AFAIK.

  • Ifoni ingadinga ikhodi yokuvula kumenzi

  • Ngena ku-fastbootoem unlock uma idinga ikhodi

  • Cabanga uma i-OEM stock firmware kufanele ikhanyiswe kusuka ku-fastboot ngaphambi kokukhanyisa i-ROM yangokwezifiso (hlola i-flashfile.xml)

  • Khanyisa ukutakulafastboot flash recovery twrp.bin

  • Qalisa ekubuyiseleni i-flash rom

  • I-Flash microG ngokusebenzisa iskripthi sokusetha esingekho emthethweni

  • I-Flash magisk

  • Isikhiphi seMagisk module ukususa amamojula abangela ama-bootloops ekululameni

  • Mhlawumbe khanyisa i-kernel yangokwezifiso

  • Hlola ukuzihlola kwe-microG ukubona ukuthi yini edinga ukulungiswa (i-apk spoofing, nlp enobunye, njll.)

  • Faka i-xposed nge-magisk

  • Lungiselela umgadli wenhlanhla

  • Faka izimakethe: f-droid, aurora, aptoide

  • Faka okunye esikhundleni sezinhlelo zokusebenza: ikhalenda-> etar, imiyalezo-> isiginali (iseshini?), Imeyili-> deltachat (noma lutho ..), ikhibhodi-> anysoftkb, amafayela-> mixplorer, browser-> bromite / firefox, youtube-> newpipe, ikhamera-> vula ikhamera, umculo-> vynil / igilamafoni, izindawo zokusebenzela-> indawo yokuhlala

  • Izinhlelo zokusebenza zebhethri: ama-betterbatterystats, misa ukushaja ibhethri (akufanele kudingeke), yenza kube lula
