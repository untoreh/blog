kumanani we-GPU

Kusho ukuthini i-MSRP?

Ngokwanda kokuhlolwa kwezimali ze-crypto izintengo ze-GPU zikhuphukele ku-2-4x zazo I-MSRP . Abanye basola i-covid kanye nokukhiya okuphoqe abantu emakhaya ukwanda kwesidingo semishini yasekhaya; noma kunjalo i- cishe 1:1 correlation between historical prices of gpus and cryptos makes covid almost irrelevant.

Kuthiwani ngokufunda komshini?

Uma kuqhathaniswa, ukufunda ngomshini (noma ikakhulukazi, ukufunda okujulile) kugcwalisa kuphela uhla lwamanani aphezulu kakhulu ngoba ludinga FP ukucacisa, okuyisidingo se-niche, lapho njenge-crypto I-GPU pow izama ukusebenzisa i- okuphelele Imiyalo ye-GPU isethiwe, ngakho-ke uhla lwemikhiqizo ephelele yemakethe ye-GPU luthandeka kwezimayini.

Singalindela ukuthi amanani we-GPU abuyele ku-MSRP?

Cha. Imakethe ye-GPU idliwe yimayini ye-crypto futhi ayisoze yalulama.

  • Ama-GPU akhona i-stackableokusho ukuthi umvukuzi wakho ojwayelekile uthenga okungenani ama-4 GPU lapho-ke njengoba umthengi wakho ojwayelekile edinga i-1 GPU kuphela ezikhathini eziningi

  • Uma ama-cryptocurrensets amakhulu eguqukela ku- POS (njenge I-ETH ), ezinye izimali ze-crypto zizothatha amandla we-hashing, ukulungiswa kwentengo ebukhali kakhulu kufanele kwenzeke ukudala ukuthengiswa okungazelelwe kwe-GPU ngabavukuzi.

  • Abavukuzi bacisha futhi basebenzise imishini yezimayini ngokuya ngesilinganiso somvuzo ophakathi kwezindleko zikagesi nezintengo ze-cryptocurrency, ngeke bathengise i-Hardware ekuqaleni.

Kwenzenjani?

Ama-GPU kuphela ngesikhathi sokubhala ngamanani afanelekile yilawa ahlanganisiwe, nama-laptops athumela ngama-GPU (kufayela le- iselula izinhlobo). Uma abakhiqizi befuna ukuvula kabusha imakethe ye-GPU kubasebenzisi abaphakathi kufanele banikeze izixazululo ezihlanganisiwe ukuncintisana kakhulu ngokumelene nabazinikele. Noma dizayina uhlobo oluhlukile lwe-GPU oluphakathi nendawo phakathi kokuzinikela nokuhlanganisiwe; Kanjani? ngokucabanga ngama-CPU ahlanganiswe ngokuqinile nama-GPU asengama-modular (ngakho-ke angashintsheka, kepha angaguquguquki!).
