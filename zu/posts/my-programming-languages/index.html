Izilimi engizikhethayo zokuhlela

Ukuze ngigcine uhlu lufushane ngizama ukunamathela kulo ulimi olulodwa nge-ecosystem

UJulia

Ok lena akuyona i-ecosystem kodwa njengoba kuwulimi engilukhetha kakhulu kufanele ize kuqala. Isizathu engisithandayo ukuthi iwukuphela kolimi olunamandla ergonomic ngempela , Ngithanda ukuthi kulula kangakanani ukulinganisa izinto, ngiyayithanda i-Makie, indlela izinhlobo ezibhala ngayo nokuthi isistimu yokuthumela ihlangana kanjani ngaphandle komthungo nezinhlobo zepharamitha.

UJulia ihweba ngenkumbulo ethile nesikhathi sokuhlanganisa ukuze kube nesivinini kanye nokusebenziseka kalula, futhi akuvamile ukubona izinqubo ezincane kuno ~500M wenqama. Ngakho-ke uma uzonda i-electron noma i-java ungase ube nenkinga no-julia futhi, nakuba zikhona izixazululo ezikunikeza amabhinari amancane we-julia wonke ayisilingo, futhi noma kunjalo, i-julia iwulimi lokuqala olunamandla, ngakho-ke ngicabanga ukuthi icala eliyinhloko lokusebenzisa okuqondiwe lizosebenza. njalo kube ukugijima izinto kusukela repl futhi binaries ihlanganiswe kuzohlala izakhamuzi yesibili, kodwa isikhathi sizosho.

Muva nje kuphume ulimi olubizwa nge-mojo, futhi abanye abantu baqala ukubuza ukuthi "kuwusongo" kujulia. Uma usebenzise i-julia isikhathi eside, bese ucwebezela phezu kwamadokhumenti e-mojo (njengoba ngenzile) kungenzeka ukuthi ubonile ukuthi i-mojo iwulimi olungaguquki. ibhowudi phezu kwe-python enamandla ngakho-ke kunzima ukuyibiza ngolimi "oluhlanganisiwe" ne-python, ngakho-ke uqhathanisa ama-apula namawolintshi...

Uma ufuna ukuqhathanisa u-julia nokunye kukhona izilimi ezimbalwa ezisuselwe ku-"JAOT":

  • i-numba: efanayo ne-julia llvm jit esekelwe ku-mixmash ye-python, i-numpy kanye nezisho ezithile ze-numba, awukwazi ngempela ukwakha amaphrojekthi amakhulu asekelwe kuyo, awusizo kuphela ekusheshiseni imisebenzi emincane.

  • I-gccemacs: ama-emacs kwimodi yokuhlanganisa yomdabu enza okufanayo ne-julia, kodwa ngendlela ebuthakathaka kakhulu, ngoba ayikwazi ukucacisa izinhlobo ezibambekayo ezivela ku-lisp, akunjalo. emacs lisp , futhi isekelwe ku-gcc jit.

Nim

Singakubiza ngokuthi "C" ecosystem, futhi ulimi lwami lwesibili engiluthandayo ngu ngim , kodwa angivamile ukuyisebenzisa ngoba ithuluzi layo liwubuwula. Ngakho-ke sicela othile alahle izigidi ezimbalwa ekuthuthukisweni kwamathuluzi e-nim, ngiyabonga! Kuyintandokazi yami ngoba njengoba ngishilo ngaphambili ujulia kunzima kakhulu ukuyithumela futhi awufuni neze ukuhambisa amabhinari angama-gigabyte. I-Nim esikhundleni salokho ingahlanganisa ama-binaries amancane kakhulu futhi yenze i-tham static nayo.

Okuwukuphela kwephuzu lobuhlungu (ngokombono wolimi ofc) ukusebenzisana phakathi kwemicu ne-async. Ukudlulisa izinto phakathi kwama-async couritines nochungechunge kungagcwala ama-gotchas, ngakho-ke ayinalo uhlobo oluthile lwesistimu eyenza kube lula ukujuquza phakathi kwemicu nesikhathi sokusebenza esingavumelanisi. Futhi ukusebenzisa isikhathi sokusebenza se-async kungenza ama-binaries akhule kakhulu ngenxa yokuvalwa kabusha kwe-async noma into efana naleyo, okubalulekile ukuthi amabhanari anomthwalo wemisebenzi ye-codegen-ed, futhi ukuyisusa futhi kunzima ngenxa yohlelo lokuqamba igama.

Ngaphandle kokuba lula ukuthunyelwa kalula, inokusekelwa okuhle kwe-ffi, i-syntax ye-dot call, isistimu ye-macro/template engcono kakhulu kune-lips (uma ungayinaki ukuntuleka kwe-sexprs), ama-macros e-julia alungile kodwa i-nim ingakwazi "ukuthumela" ama-macros. nezifanekiso phezu kwezinhlobo, zikuvumela ukuthi ukhethe phakathi "kokungcola nokuhlanzeka" futhi iseva yolimi ikwazile ukuhlinzeka ngobuhlakani obuningi phakathi kwemisebenzi, ama-macros kanye nezifanekiso ngisho nokugqwala okungakwazi ukukufeza (uma nje bekungeke kuphahlazeke njalo ngemizuzwana emi-2 futhi ube nesineke njengesihogo ... phefumula).

I-Nim nayo ine-nimscript, isifiso sami ukuthi i-repl esebenzisanayo ephelele ingakhiwa eduze kwalokho, uma ukulayishwa kabusha kwekhodi eshisayo kungase kuthathwe futhi, okungukuthi...

Ukugqwala

Ukugqwala iyona engiyisebenzisayo njengoba i-nim tooling iwubukhazikhazi, ukube i-nim tooling bekungeyona into engalungile bengizosebenzisa i-nim. Mina ngokwami ​​angikutholi ukuphepha kwenkumbulo yokugqwala kunenzuzo efana ne-nim. I-Nim ayinayo "i-concurrency engesabi", lokho kuqhathaniswa nokugqwala kulula ku-nim ukuya ku-segfaul lapho usebenza ngochungechunge. Noma kunjalo ukugqwala kusebenzisa isikhathi sokuphila ukuze kuzuzwe ukuphepha kwentambo, kuyilapho i-nim ingenayo into enjalo ezingeni lolimi. Izikhathi zokuphila cishe ziyisici esididayo nesinzima kakhulu solimi lokugqwala ngakho kungase kube nesizathu lapho...

Ngokuvamile ungasho ukuthi ngemva kwephuzu elithile izimbungulu zebhizinisi ziba zivame kakhulu kunezimbungulu zememori, futhi awufuni ukwenza inkimbinkimbi yolimi ukuze uzuze ukuncipha kwembuyiselo ekuphepheni kwememori ngenkathi uhlanganisa ukufundeka nokwandisa ubunkimbinkimbi bolimi ngokwalo. Ngenxa yokuthi ukulinganisa ukuthi lungakanani ulimi olwenza izimbungulu zebhizinisi zivame kakhulu akunakwenzeka ukubalwa, leli phuzu lizohlala lingenangqondo.

Okunye

Lezi ulimi kuphela olungaphezulu kohlu lwami lwezilimi "ezihlukile" enginazo amandla khetha kanye nge-ecosystem

  • I-Beam: Ukukhetha okunzima phakathi kwe-gleam ne-elixir, ingase igcine ikhethe i-gleam noma izame zombili bese ukhetha eyodwa, ngaphandle kokuthi ziyizilimi ezihluke kakhulu, kodwa ngishilo ukuthi kungakhetha olulodwa kuphela ohlelweni lwe-ecosystem.

Okulandelayo yilokhu engingakusebenzisa noma yiziphi lezi zinhlelo zemvelo, kodwa angikazizami ngempela:

  • I-JVM/Graal: I-Clojure. Isekhondi eliseduze kungenzeka ukuthi laliyi-scala esikhathini esidlule, kodwa i-clojure ukuphela kwenkulumo "yezimboni" lapho, ngakho-ke kufanele ukhethe leyo.

  • I-Node/Deno/Bun: Ngingakhetha umbhalo wokubhala ngoba nje uwulimi oluvame kakhulu lokuhlanganisa ukuya ku-js. Ngokuzayo ngingacabanga nge-ReScript, uma i-ecosystem yayo izoke ikhule ngokwanele.

  • CLR: Akekho? Ngingathi F# kodwa ngingathanda ukuthi cha CLR. I-clr ibonakala inokusebenzisa i-clojure ne-swift ngakho-ke kukhona lokho, kodwa umbono wami ubulokhu uwukuthi ukusebenzisa noma iyiphi yalezi "chweba" kuzohlangatshezwa nenqwaba yokungahambisani, noma kunjalo angiziboni ngike ngasondela ku-clr ukukhetha komuntu siqu.

  • Lisp: ingabe i-lisp i-ecosystem? Eh...akunjalo, nakuba omunye umuntu engase athi konke kungokwasexprs futhi ukuthuthwa kwezinto kusuka kuhlelo olulodwa kuya kwesinye kuseduze njengombuzo wosesho nokufaka esikhundleni njengoba uzowuthola. Ukube i-emacs lisp ibinolimi lwesigodi olusheshayo olungasebenzisa ama-gccemacs ukuthi akhiphe ikhodi esheshayo bengizoyicabangela ngokuqinile ngaphezu kolimi oluvamile. Uma bekufanele ngikhethe isikimu esikhundleni salokho, lokho bekungaba irakhethi ngaphandle kwemibuzo. Ngabe ngizibona ngibhala isoftware yangempela ku-commonlisp noma ku-racket? Akwenzeki, ngoba uma bengidinga i-lisp bengizokhetha i-clojure.

Ngaphandle kwalezi zikhetho zolimi "ze-ecosystem" kukhona ezinye ezimbalwa okufanele zikhulunywe ngokuhloniphekile.

  • I-Raku: Ngenze i-PHPing emaphakathi esikhathini esidlule, futhi i-zero perl, nokho i-raku ibonakala ithakazelisa ngempela, uma kungeyona i-code-golfability engenakuqhathaniswa yolimi.

  • Okubomvu: isithembiso esigcwele sesitaki siyathandeka, futhi ifa le-rebol lihlukile ngokwanele kangangokuthi kufanelekile ukubhala uhlelo lokusebenza olunosayizi ohloniphekile ukuze uzame

  • I-Dart: Uma ngikhubeka kwenye i-UI dev ngingayizama, kodwa ngingathanda ukunamathela kolunye lwezilimi zami eziphezulu ngisho ne-UI dev, umbuzo ube usuba, ngamalini ingabe i-dart (noma i-haxe!) yandisa umkhiqizo ngenxa yosekelo lwezingxenyekazi eziningi?

  • Ivesi: ulimi olusuka kumidlalo eyingqophamlando, luhlukile ngenxa yokuba ukuphela kwendlela yesimanjemanje yolimi lwe-prolog-ish. Kubonakala kucace ukuthi i-epic yakha lokhu ngoba wonke umuntu uzonda amapulani e-EU. Kepha uma ulimi luhlala luqinile ku-UE angiziboni ngike ngaluzama.

Ziningi ezinye izilimi ezidumile, njengezinye ezisebenzayo ezifana ne-ocaml, i-haskell noma ezinye ezilula njenge-zig noma i-vlang engingazibalanga ngoba ngicabanga ukuthi isethi efile kakhulu ngaphezu kwe-paradigm (ukungaguquki/ukuba lula), futhi ngokuvamile ngicabanga ukuthi ngincamela izilimi "eziyindilinga".

Lolu hlu lugcine lungelufushane kangako, ngiyaxolisa.
