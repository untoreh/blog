Emalini yokwehla kwamandla emali

Yini edala ukwehla kwamandla emali ezimakethe zonxiwankulu.

Manje sesiqonda kangcono ukuthi kuncane kangakanani esikuqondayo mayelana nokwehla kwamandla emali

Njengoba izimboni zibuyela kuhulumeni ogcwele (noma eduze kwawo) ngemuva kokusatshalaliswa kwemithi yokugomela i-covid, bekulindeleke ukuthi amanani entengo abuyele endaweni yawo.

Noma ubani owenza lokhu kucabangela ufunde kuphela ngezimakethe, futhi akakaze abambe iqhaza kweyodwa.

Imithetho yemakethe

  • Ngaphandle kwenzuzo, azikho izimakethe. Lona umcabango oyisisekelo, akekho osebenza mahhala. Lokhu kungase kuzwakale kusobala, kodwa nginesiqiniseko sokuthi kukhona umuntu oqhelelene neqiniso ukuze acabange ukuthi abantu bangahle “baphile” nje. Cha, ingxenye "esanda kusinda" ikufikisa nje kokuthi "akukho ukuvukela".

  • Uma ungenza inzuzo eyengeziwe, uyakwenza lokho.

Umphumela

Kwenzekani ngemva kokuba usungene esikhathini sokulahlekelwa? Ufuna ukukhokhela lokho kulahlekelwa, ukuze unyuse amanani. Ngemuva kokuthi ukhuphule amanani ukuze uphinde uthole inzuzo...kungani ukwehlisa?

inertia + profits == Inflation

...Kodwa linda...kukhona okunye!

I-domino ye-Supply chain

Ukwenyusa amanani kuyinto hhayi iphuzu ngesikhathi. Ukwenyusa amanani kusuka kumlingisi oyedwa ochungechungeni lokunikezela kubangela imiphumela ye-domino, yonke indawo engezansi kufanele ithwale izindleko noma iziphushele endaweni ephansi.[1] , kuze kufike i-node yokugcina (umthengi). Ukuze ungadideki igcwele izimo, njengokukhuphuka kwentengo yamandla, okuthinta wonke ama-node ngesikhathi esisodwa.

sum(loss(node) for node in source..consumer) == Inflation

Iziprofetho ezizigcwalisayo

Ukulindela ukwehla kwamandla emali kubangela ukwehla kwamandla emali. Uma abezindaba bekhuluma nge-inflation "engenzeka", kuzokwenzeka, njengoba wonke umuntu ezozama zilungiselele kusengaphambili ngoku...nyusa izintengo nokwenza inzuzo eyengeziwe.

loss(t+1) -> Inflation(t)

Isixuku

Kwenzekani lapho iqembu labantu lenyusa intengo yempahla? abanye ababambiqhaza bangenza okufanayo ngomphumela kontanga. Izinqola ezizenzakalelayo bayazalwa! Akudalwanga yinoma ubani ngempela, ngamandla angaphandle nje, kungenzeka kuphela ukuhlakazwa ngamanye amandla angaphandle anamandla alinganayo.

(profits(b) + 1 -> profits(B - {b}) + 1 for every b in B) -> inflation(B)

Uphi umncintiswano!?

Ukuncintisana kuyinto hhayi okunikeziwe.

  • Izimakethe ezisafufusa zinokuqhudelana (okuningi) okuphezulu, kuyilapho zonke izimakethe ezisunguliwe zigcina ziphenduke ama-monopolies noma ama-duopolies. Umnotho omile (njengomnotho wangemuva kwe-covid) unezimakethe ezimbalwa ezisafufusa.

  • Ezinye izimakethe zidinga imali eningi yangaphambili ukuze zihehe ukuncintisana.

  • Ukuncintisana kuvame ukuvela ekulindelweni okuphusile kwenzuzo (isb. ngenxa yobuchwepheshe obungcono, ukuhlelwa kwezinto, njll.), hhayi emalini engafanele eyenziwa ababambe iqhaza.

Ingena kanjani i-QE/QT kulokhu?

Ngelinye ilanga ngiyethemba ukuqonda ukuthi ukulawula ukuhlinzekwa kwezimali komnotho kufanele "kulungise izinto". Kimina kubukeka kanjena:

  • Inkinobho yokwethuka : uma ungazi ukuthi wenzeni, bhuqa[2] imali, bekufanele yenze okuthile!

  • Isando : lapho yonke inkinga yezomnotho, isipikili.

Noma kunjalo, ukuze kubhekwane nokwehla kwamandla emali, ibhange elikhulu (lesaba ukwehliswa kwenani lemali) linquma ukwenza QT. Lokhu kusho ukuthi i-liquidity iyancipha, kukhona ukuboleka okuncane, imali encane iyakhushulwa, bese kulandela ukwehla komnotho. Ngokungena kwemali encane, ukuncintisana okuncane kujoyina izimakethe, ababambe iqhaza abanaso isizathu sokwehlisa amanani, ukwehla kwamandla emali kuhlala kungathinteki. I-QT, ngenhloso yokuvikela inani lemali, ine kuphindwe kabili inzuzo eyengeziwe eyenziwe abenzi bokwehla kwamandla emali.

[1]Kuleli phuzu kufanele ujwayelene ne imithetho yezimakethe
[2]Kungenzeka ukuthi awufundanga kahle njengokuthi "i-raffle", nami kulungile.
