Ama-Emac

Kungani ama-emacs, nohlu lwami lwama-emacs uqobo.

Yini ama-emacs?

A umshini obonakalayo noma utolika we emacs ulimi lokuhlela i-lisp ... uhlobo lwe. I-emacs lisp programming language (elisp) yinhloso ejwayelekile, kepha inokwesekwa kwekilasi lokuqala komhleli wombhalo oyisebenzisayo. Izinhlobo zokuqala zigxile ekuhleleni umbhalo, noma kunjalo ungabhala noma yini oyifunayo ngokuhle ukusebenza okuhle (muva nje ngiyabonga ukuhlanganiswa kwe-jit ) ngoba iyinzalo ye-lisp ye- MACLISP nengane yakini ye i-lisp ejwayelekile . Kodwa-ke i-emacs lisp ayinayo indinganiso, futhi imininingwane ilingana nokuqaliswa okudume kakhulu, lokho kungama-GNU Emacs.

Kungani ama-emac?

Ngenkulumo ethi "Emacs is a operative system" abantu basho ukuthi ungayisebenzisa ukwenza noma yini ongayisebenzisela ikhompyutha. Iyasilela ekubeni uhlelo lokusebenza ngokoqobo ngoba i-kernel, (imvamisa i-linux) isaphethe i-hardware.[1] Kungabizwa kalula ngokuthi indawo yedeskithophu ngoba ihlinzeka ngemvelo yokusebenza nayo. Kukunika amandla wokubhala ikhodi esheshayo yokuxhumana ukuze uxhumane nanoma yiluphi uhlelo lokusebenza, kungangcola njengama-Shell script noma kucatshangwe kahle ukuhlinzeka ngama-API azinzile. Empeleni imisebenzi eminingi yama-emac inikezwa ngamaphakeji.[2]Ngabe abanye abahleli banganwebeka njengama-emac? Cha ngoba? Ngoba abanye abahleli basebenzisa imodeli ehlukile yomsebenzisi, eyodwa lapho umsebenzisi kufanele avinjelwe ngoba kubhekwa "njengesivakashi" sendawo esebenzayo enikeza "isevisi" kumsebenzisi. Lokhu akunjalo ngama-emacs, lapho imvelo nomsebenzisi befana. Kuma-emac, uyakwazi ukufinyelela kuyo yonke into, futhi ungaguqula cishe zonke izinto ngezindlela ezifanayo lezo zinto ezadalwa ngazo, aka elisp.

Ingabe kuwufanele?

Izinzuzo zikhona, kungukutshalwa kwemali, futhi njenganoma yikuphi ukutshalwa kwezimali, kufanele ulindele ukuzuza ngokulingana nokuthi ufaka malini, ngakho-ke kufanele uchithe isikhathi esithile ekufundeni nasekusebenzeni ukuthi ukusisebenzisa kanjani.

Uhlu lwami lwezifiso ze-GNU

Ama-Emac uhlelo oluhle kakhulu lokusebenza, kepha isihleli sombhalo esibi.

... Noma okuthile ngaleyo migqa. Ukuqaliswa kwe-GNU Emacs, njengama-emacs, kunezimpande zayo emashumini ayisishiyagalombili, futhi izingxenye eziningi zomnyombo wazo zibonisa iminyaka yabo ...

  • I-dicothomy phakathi kwesibonisi sokuqhafaza ne-interface esibulalayo. Njengoba i-emacs indala iqale njengohlelo lokusebenza ukusebenzisa ngaphakathi kwe-terminal kepha ngokufika kwezikhombimsebenzisi zokuqhafaza eminyakeni yamashumi ayisishiyagalolunye, yaqala ukuqoqa ukusebenza okulungiselelwe kakhulu iwindi lisuselwe ukusebenzisana kanye nango-2000 ukusekelwa kwe- X11 umthetho olandelwayo wethulwa. Njengoba iminyaka idlula, ukuba lusizo kwenguqulo yokugcina kungancipha, futhi ngayo, intshisekelo yokuyigcina, ngoba ukuba nama-emacs kuziphatha ngendlela ehlukile kuye ngokuqhafaza / ukuphela kungumthwalo, futhi kuyinkimbinkimbi. Ngingathanda ukubona ukukhishwa kwe-emacs GUI ngaphezulu kwento efana ne- isithuphaumthetho olandelwayo, onjengokuthi izindawo zokuqhafaza nezokugcina zingafinyelela ezingeni elijulile lokwabelana ngamakhodi. Lokhu kuzodinga ama-emac ukuguqula umcabango we- "terminal first, graphical second" (okuthi ngesikhathi sokubhala ngicabanga ukuthi usabambile).[3]

  • Ikhodi ye-Async ku-elisp akulula, noma kungenzeka kwezinye izimo. Ama-Emacs asekela imicu Veza lokho kungama isikhashana futhi kubuyisele isilawuli esikhulu, okuwukuqalisa kwe- ukubambisana okuningi . Ngabe ama-emacs adinga ukuxhaswa kwesimanje kwama-async primitives? Iqiniso lokuthi ama-emacs akhawulelwe kakhulu kumgogodla owodwa kusho ukuthi ikhodi isetshenziswe kahle, futhi ama-emac ngokwawo angasebenza ngokushesha okukhulu kumadivayisi amancane. Ngicabanga ukuthi ama-emac, ekubeni isikhombimsebenzisi esibonakalayo, kufanele asekele kuphela i- isikhombimsebenzisi sokuqhafaza asynchronous . Kulokhu ubukhulu kanye nomongo wekhodi ye-asynchronous kuzovinjelwa ku-UI kuphela. Lona ngumklamo osetshenziswe kwabanye abahleli abasuselwa kuma-electron njenge I-VSCode , lapho i-UI isebenza ngenqubo yayo ehlukile. Ngingathanda ikakhulukazi i- ukuthayipha isipiliyoni ukungaphazanyiswa, futhi ngidayisa kahle ngokuqokomisa ukubambezeleka kwe-syntax, ukuqeda, noma okunye ukusebenzisa amathuluzi okusiza isikhathi sangempela esithambile ukubambezeleka kokufaka.

  • Kusukela kuma-emacs obekufanele ube nokufinyelela ku- konke , indlela ama-emacs akhuluma ngayo i-X11 ihaha impela njengoba ama-emac acishe asebenzise kabusha iseva ephelele ye-X11 ngaphakathi kwayo. Lokhu kudale ezinye izinkinga ngokomlando[4] ngoba izikhathi eziningi ama-emacs asetshenziswa njengewindi ngaphakathi kwenye i-X11, hhayi i-an Iseva yeX11 uqobo. Iseva yokubonisa, ephatha amaseshini wokuqhafaza. Ngabe ibukeka kanjani imvelo ehlukile yokubonisa? Ngicabanga okuthile okuqala kusuka ezingeni eliphansi, futhi kubukeke kufana nenjini yomdlalo, mhlawumbe kusekelwe I-SDL . Ngaphezulu kokukhishwa kwevidiyo okuqondile okuyisisekelo, lokho kungafinyeleleka kusuka ku-elisp (i-natively compiled) noma i-API ezinzile yeC (noma yokugqwala). Uhlelo lwama-emacs luzosebenza ngaphezulu kwe- "pixel cruncher" backend futhi lungabe lusathembela kuthuluzi lamathuluzi lokuqhafaza langaphandle njengelucid noma I-GTK futhi sifinyelele izinga eliphakeme le-agnosticism yesikhulumi. Kungasho nokusebenza okuthembekile kuzo zonke izinhlelo, ngoba lapho ukubonisa kusingathwa ukusebenza kwamathuluzi wokuqhafaza wangaphandle kungahle kungalindelekile, futhi ngokwesibonelo izinto ezifana kwakha ifreyimu entsha bagcina kancane emafasiteleni kune-linux; lapho lokhu kusebenza kusetshenziswa kaningi, njengokhetho lokuqedela ukubonisa ukungezwani kuyabonakala.[5]

  • Ukuba nezinkundla zokubonisa zesimanjemanje ngeke kuvumele ukusebenza okungcono nje kuphela, kepha ukufinyelela kumathuluzi wokubuka athuthuke kakhulu, njengokushintshwa okufanele, izithunzi, ukufiphala, kanye nohlelo olungcono lwezendlalelo, ngoba ama-emacs njengamanje azisebenzisa imbondela esikala kabi. Ngokuvamile, kungaba kuhle ukuba namandla aphelele we-CSS3 nangaphezulu, ukulungele ukusebenzisa nokuthuthukisa izixhumano zamanje ze-spartan emacs.[6]

  • Sebenzela ukucaciselwa okuthile kwe-elisp. Abanakekeli be-emacs bamanje banakekela ikhono lokusebenzisa ama-emac kuzingxenyekazi ezahlukahlukene (izakhiwo ze-cpu). Uma umnyombo omningi ubhalwa kabusha ku-elisp uqobo[7], okuwukuphela kwento esele kubangaphakathi iba ngutolika ongaconsi phansi. Ngokuvumelana kolunye uhlobo lokucaciswa kwe-elisp, ukufaka wonke ama-emacs kusikhathi sokusebenza esihlukile kuyenzeka, ngoba inkinga ilingana nokusebenzisa nje "utolika" we-elisp.

  • Iplatifomu yezimali ye-emacs? Kulokhu okubhalwe kabusha kuyakhulunywa ukuthi kunzima kanjani "ukuxhasa nje" abantu ukuze basebenze ezintweni, futhi kufanele kube ngabathuthukisi abanentshisekelo yokusebenza kuzici ezithile okufanele zicele uxhaso. Lokhu kepha akubheki iqiniso lokuthi ababaningi abantu abazi kahle ama-emacs angaphakathi. Ngokusebenzayo, ukulinda onjiniyela ukuthola ulwazi lokusebenzisa izici ezithile kungathatha iminyaka engama-20 (nokubala); yini ezosheshisa izinto kungaba kuqala khokhela abantu ukuthi bafunde (futhi babhale imibhalo!) mayelana nama-emacs angaphakathi ukuze abantu abaningi bazijwayeze futhi bathuthe kancane kancane ukuthuthukiswa kwama-emac i-cathedral ne-bazaar imodeli yokuthuthukela kokunye okubandakanya kakhulu.

  • Uhlu lwezifiso:

    • Hlala esihlahleni zonke izinto! Ukugqamisa kwe-Emacs syntax kusekelwe ezincwadini ezijwayelekile, ezingasheshi (iningi lezikhathi) kepha kungadala amawa wokusebenza angafiseleka ukukugwema ngokuphelele. Kepha umuthi wezihlahla awusizi nje ngokugqamisa kwe-syntax, unikeza ukumelwa okulandelanayo kokuqukethwe kwe-buffer okuzuzisa amanye amaphakheji futhi:

      • i-smartparens, i-paredit, i-lispyville, njll ...: Leli yiphakethe elihlinzeka nge-api efakwa kakhulu yokusebenzisa ubuqili bombhalo ngokususelwa ezintweni zombhalo, njengokushwibeka, kepha kuhamba kancane (ama-smartparens ikakhulukazi). Ukuphinda ubuyise i-api ngaphezulu kwesihlahla somuthi kubukeka kuyathandeka.

      • I-Polymode: imodi encane yokuxuba izindlela ezahlukahlukene ezinkulu ku-buffer efanayo. Kusebenza isifunda sinciphise izinkulumo ezijwayelekile, ukusebenzisa isihlahla lapha kuzosiza ukusheshisa izinto.

      • I-Orgmode: ngemuva kokubhalwa kohlelo lolimi lokuhlalisa izihlahla, i-orgmode ingathola izigaxa zesivinini esikhulu ngoba umqondo omningi we-org mode kanye nokwenziwa kusekelwe kokuqukethwe kwebhafa okufanele kudluliswe njalo (izakhiwo, amabhlokhi wekhodi, izihloko, okukhishwayo)

    • Faka ihaba ukuya ku-orgmode: lawa maphakheji asebenzisa isixhumanisi emhlabeni jikelele, kanti ama-orgmode hyperlink asebenza kuphela phakathi kwama-orgmode buffers. Futhi i-orgmode esekwe kumodi yohlaka, ingahlomula kwimodi ye-kline enikezwe yi-hyperbole, esebenzisa ama-node angashayisani.

    • ICirchat: igama mashup phakathi isandi.el futhi weechat.el . I-Circe isebenzisa i-UI enhle kakhulu yama-buffers engxoxo, ngenkathi i-weechat.el inikeza ukwesekwa kokuhlangana ne- umthetho olandelwayo we-weechat , kepha ine-UI empofu nephutha. Sebenzisa i-weechat ukuphatha ukuxhumana kwezingxoxo kukhulula ama-emacs ekuphatheni konke ukuxhumana kwe-IRC nokuqapha wonke ama-buffers, anganciphisa ama-emacs kakhulu. Ngaphezu kwalokho i-weechat inokusekelwa kwe-plugin ye-matrix, ngakho-ke uthola ukwesekwa okuqondile kwe-matrix ngaphakathi kwama-emac nawo.

    • I-Tramp: tramp yiphakheji ye-emacs ephatha ukuxhumana okukude, kepha ihamba kancane. Lokhu akukhona ukuthi wonke amaphutha we-tramp, ngoba amanye amaphakheji awabheki ukuthi iseshini yamanje ingeyasendaweni noma ikude, futhi ifaka izinto ezinjengokusebenza okunzima kohlelo lwefayela kuma-endpoints akude. Kepha ukunyathela nakho akunakho ukuxhaswa kwe-async, okuyisimo esidabukisa kakhulu sento ethile ekhipha imiyalo kuzinqubo zangaphandle eziphatha imigomo eceliwe (ssh, sftp, docker ...). Isipiliyoni se-VSCode ngokususa abasingathi singcono kakhulu, futhi ama-emacs adinga ukuqinisa umdlalo wawo :)
