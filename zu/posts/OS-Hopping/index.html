I-OS Hopping

Isifinyezo esifushane sezinhlelo zokusebenza engizisebenzisile

Lapho ngithola ikhompyutha yami yokuqala eza ne-Windows XP (eneminyaka engu-13y). Ngemuva kokuxakaniseka okumbalwa bekufanele kufundwe ukufaka kabusha amawindi ... Kwakungeyona ipulagi futhi idlale njenganamuhla ... Isipiliyoni sami sokuqala nge-linux sasine-PCLinuxOS okusobala ukuthi usaqhubeka njengoba inikela ngokufakwa okugcwele kokuqhafaza. Ngemuva kwalokho ngiqonde ngqo I-Arch Linux . Ngidlale ne-Arch Linux isikhashana (njengokwenza i-gnome UI ibukeke njengeMacOS noma iWindows Vista ama-gnome bheka ama-mods ). Ngemuva kweminyaka ethile ngabuyela eLinux, okokuqala ngazama I-Alpine , kepha izinqolobane zayo zazingagculisi (okwamanje?) ukuhlolwa repo).

Ngibe sengizama I-Linux engekho isikhashana, kepha bengisebenzisa i- i-musl version ebingenawo amaphakheji amahle wokusekelwa ahlakaniphile, ngakho-ke bengisebenzisa i-void linux plus zokusebenza ezitsheni ku i3wm imvelo esekelwe. Kepha izinhlelo zokusebenza zemidwebo ezihlanganisiwe zazingakasakazeki, izixazululo ezinjenge-flatpak snapd noma i-firejail zazingakasakazeki noma zizinzile, ngakho-ke ngishintshele ku-Ubuntu, ngasetha i-ZFS RAID-Z ( ephila ngendlela eyingozi ) ukufakwa kwezimpande. Njengezindawo zedeskithophu bengisagcina i3wm, bese ngiya ku- zanele bangela i-wayland, bese i-KDE plasma ngoba bengingasakwazi ukuzihlupha ngodoti wamafayela weyunithi ehleliwe okufanele abe umsebenzisi ephethwe ukuthola nje ukusethwa okuncane okulindelwe ukusebenza nganoma yimaphi ama-WM wothayela.

I-KDE yinhle, futhi ngiyithatha njengonqenqemeni oluphuma egazini lwezindawo zedeskithophu, kepha futhi inkulu, futhi inezinhlelo zokusebenza eziningi ezishoda ngokulungiswa okuhle, ngakho-ke iseluleko sami ukuthi ungazami kanzima ukunamathela kumvelo we-K * kuphela we-GUI futhi vele usebenzise okusebenzayo, i-KDE inokusekela okuhle kwesitayela se-GTK. I-KWin inezinketho ezinhle kakhulu zokwenza ngokwezifiso futhi ungabopha ama-hotkey ukuyenza iziphathe kahle kakhulu ku-a ukuntanta kuqala kuthayipha i-WM. Uma ufuna i- kuqala-tile ukuthayipha i-WM lapho kunezandiso ezimbalwa, kepha ezingasebenzi kahle kwi-wayland (ngesikhathi sokubhala).

Muva nje ngibuyele ku-Windows (10) .. futhi , hhayi ngokuyinhloko ngoba imidlalo njengoba kusamele ngithole isikhathi nesisusa sokudlala futhi, kepha ngifisa ukwazi ukuthi a WSL imvelo ye-linux esekwe nokuhlanganiswa okuhle kwezinhlelo zokusebenza zokuqhafaza ( WSLg). Ngesikhathi sokubhala, ngokudabukisayo Windows 10 isipiliyoni ukwehla kwe-de-facto kusuka ku-linux KDE. IWindows ne igundane centric I-UX ibonakala ingenalutho oluqondiswe ekwenzeni ngokwezifiso izinqamuleli zekhibhodi ezijulile. Akucaci ukuthi ngabe Isiko leWindows izinqamuleli zibanjiwe, njengoba kubonakala njengembobo yezokuphepha noma kumane kungumkhawulo onzima wegobolondo lewindows, okuphoqa ukwenziwa ngokwezifiso ngokukhishwa kwerejista. UPowertoys kusenendlela ende okufanele ayenze ukwanelisa zonke izidingo zomuntu ofuna ukulingana indawo yakhe nemikhuba yakhe (angiqiniseki ukuthi ngingabhekwa njengomsebenzisi wamandla ..). Noma kunjalo ngikwazi ukuguquguquka futhi ngezinhlelo ezimbalwa ezivela eceleni imvelo iye yamukeleka ngandlela thile.
