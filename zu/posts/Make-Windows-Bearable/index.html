Yenza iWindows Bearable

Uhlu lwamathuluzi wokwenza iWindows izwakale njengeLinux

Njengoba mina kushintshelwe kumawindi 10 nge-WSL Ngibone ukuthi ngizokwenza uhlu lwesoftware ebengizolusebenzisa ukusondeza amawindi eduze kokuhamba komsebenzi kwami ​​okujwayelekile kwe-linux. Masikucacise ukuthi iWindows UI iyabamba, inamathele kufayela le-90s futhi yonke into kufanele ichofozwe futhi ihudulwe ngesikhombi sakho segundane. Angazi noma ngabe i- isiko thing (windows devs kumele athande igundane kakhulu), noma i- inani labantu okubhekiswe kulo ("chofoza kuleyo nto!" kulula ukuyichaza), noma ngenxa ye- ukuphepha umkhuba omuhle kakhulu (uma sinika umsebenzisi ukulawula okuningi, i-malware izokusebenzisa kabi) noma i- I-Windows Shell ukwenziwa inqwaba yezikweletu zobuchwepheshe ezenza kube nzima ukwengeza ukusebenza kwabasebenzisi abaphambili. Iqiniso ukuthi izinto ezilula ku-Linux zidinga izixazululo eziqinisekisiwe ku-Windows.

Ama-Desktops abonakalayo

Ku-linux yonke imenenja yamawindows inendlela ethile yedeskithophu ebonakalayo, amawindi akhiwe ngokwamakhompiyutha abonakalayo muva nje nge-W10. Into ukuthi ngesikhathi sokubhala ungajikeleza kuphela ngakwesokunxele nangakwesokudla ngama-hotkey, futhi awekho ama-hotkey okuya kudeskithophu X. Kukhona lokhu vd umtapo wolwazi kepha angitholanga isoftware eyisebenzisayo, esikhundleni se-AHK kuphela[1] imibhalo enokusetshenziswa okungalungile okufana ne- "loop size sibe ku-VD efanele".

Ukushoda kwezinqamuleli ezifanele zama-desktop desktops angenze ngaphoqeleka ukusebenzisa i-3rd party software, okukhona okuningi, engikukhethile i-dexpot njengoba lenza zonke izinto engizodinga nokuningi (ngingajabula nge-justgo to X futhimove window to X izinqamuleli). Inkinga kuphela ukuthi i-dexpot ayikwazi ukubopha okhiye asebevele baboshwe ... ngandlela-thile i-AHK iyakwazi ukubhala ngaphezulu okhiye abasetshenziswa ezinye izinhlelo zokusebenza, kanti i-dexpot ayenzi, ngicabanga ukuthi kungenxa yokuthi incike kwi-windows API ehlukile (noma [dll]). Ukusebenzela ukubuyisela izinqamuleli engikufunayo (isb.Win+1 ) ngesinqamuleli samahhala esingasethwa ku-dexpot (isb.Win+Shift+F1)

Imephu yekhibhodi

UPowertoys ivumela ukwenza amamephu okhiye, nezinqamuleli, ukuze ngikwazi ukwenza imephu ku-CapsLock-> LeftControl naku-RightControl-> LeftControl. Futhi izinqamuleli ezifanaWin+hjkl imicibisholo. Kubi kakhuluWin+l isinqamuleli esizenzakalelayo samawindi sokukhiya isikrini futhi asikwazi ukuphinda simiswe kabusha ... ukhiye wokubhalisa ukhipha izinqamuleli bese ulungisa lokhu ... uze uvuselele amawindi ... ngakho-ke kufanele usebenzise ukulungiswa ebhuthini ngalinye!

Isiqalisi

Isiqalisi sezinhlelo zokusebenza esihlinzekwe ngama-powertoys sisebenza kakhulu, asisebenzisi imemori ethe xaxa kune-KRunner futhi siyashesha futhi siyaphendula. Isikhalazo kuphela ukuthi ngezikhathi ezithile ilahla ukugxila, kepha lokhu kungenzeka kube yinkinga yokulawulwa kweWindows (njengokulawula okuhlanekezelwe) ukuthi iliphi iwindi okufanele ligxilwe nganoma yisiphi isikhathi.

Ngishintshe kusuka ekushintsheni ngaya ku-KDE ku-Linux, ngakho-ke bengingeyona kangako isithandi samawindi asethayili. Noma kunjalo isantula into ebaluleke kakhulu eyenza ukuthayela kube wusizo, okungukuthi imithetho ukufaka isicelo kumawindi afanayo, kepha lokhu kuzodinga nokusekelwa kwama-desktops abonakalayo ... futhi sesivele sishilo isimo sabo samanje. Ku-Comparison KWin ikuvumela ukufanisa windows nezincazelo eziyinkimbinkimbi kakhulu. Amaphakheji

I-WSL / g

Ngikhuthazelwe ukushintshela kumawindi kusuka kwakamuva WSLg buyekeza. Okudinga ukuthi abashayeli be-mesa bahlanganiswe ned3d12 ukusekela emuva kweopengl . IWindows inikeza ungqimba lomphakathi lokubuka kuqala umphakathi, kepha ngakhetha i- i-arch linux ngoba bekuvele kukhona iphakethe le-AUR le- mesa nge-d3d12 . Njengoba i-WSL okwamanje ingasekeli uhlelo, ngagcina ngisebenzisa umphathi ukuphatha izinsizakalo ezimbalwa. Ukuqinisekisa ukuthi umphathi uyasebenza kunesheke kuphrofayela ye-Shell yefayela lokukhiya okufanele lakhiwe kulotmpfs uma umphathi eseqalisiwe phambilini.

I-WSL isekela kuphelaext4 file system, ukusebenzisa amanye amasistimu wefayela, kufanele ukhweze ukwahlukanisa (noma idiski) ngaphakathi ngaphakathi kwe-WSL VM. Kodwa-ke kufanele zifakwe ngesandla njengomlawulikusuka kumawindi. Ukuzenzekelayo lokhu singasebenzisa isihleli somsebenzi esivumela ukweqa ukwaziswa kwe-UAC ngokwenza imisebenzi nge-priviledge ephezulu kakhulu . Singasebenza i-wsl--mount umyalo wokufaka amadiski / ukwahlukaniswa kwethu okuthandayo bese ugijima ukufaka isistimu yefayela kusuka ngaphakathi kwe-linux. Ngoba eyami/home/ uhlala endaweni egibelebtrfs uhlelo lwefayela kufanele ngilukhuphule ngokuzenzakalela, ngakho-ke sisebenzisa/etc/fstab ukumaka ukwahlukanisa kwethu (ngeLABEL ) ukuze/home . Ngoba sisebenzisa i-nix ngenix-env sidinga ukubopha ukukhweza isitolo sethu se-nix ku-/nix kukho konke ukuqala kwe-wsl, futhi ngoba/tmp ayikhotmpfs kufanele sakhe intaba yokumbondelana enyukayotmpfs ngaphezulu kokukhona/tmp isiqondisi, ukugcina amafayela abalulekile, ikakhulukazi amafayela we-X11, adingekayo ukukhuluma neseva ye-X.

Lezi zintaba zisebenza ngombhalo, okufanele usebenze ngemuva sifake idiski ngaphakathi kwe-linux. Sidinga umsebenzi osebenza ngemuva kokuthi umsebenzi wokuphothula usuphothuliwe, futhi ngenxa yokuthi i-WSL isebenzisa ama-VM ahlukile kubasebenzisi abahlukahlukene bewindows iskripthi kufanele senziwe HHAYI ngamalungelo aphakeme (uma kungenjalo sizokhwela ku-Administrator VM).

Ukuqinisekisa ukuthi i-WSLg iyasebenza, kufanele siqiniseke ukuthi i-XDG UKUGIJIMA I-DIR isethiwe, ngoba yehlukile futhi itholakala (ngokuzenzakalela) ku-/mnt/wlsg/runtime-dir . Le yindlela amawindows angaphansi kokubukeka kwe-wayland, ungabona ukuthi njengoba imihlobiso yewindow ye-wayland ingadonselwa umqambi noma uhlelo lokusebenza, kugcina ukumiswa kwetimu yakho ye-GTK / QT, ngakho-ke ugcina ngewindi lendabuko le-linux elibukeka ngewindi ngaphakathi kwegobolondo leWindows ... okuphazamisa kancane ekuqaleni.

Emulator yesikhumulo

Ku-linux bengikusebenzisa ikati , ngoba ibingenye yama-emulators amasha wokugcina ane-GPU ukushesha[2]kanye nemodi ye-daemon eqinile evumela amawindi amaningi ngesimo esifanayo. Kodwa-ke kumawindi, ngadabula ngama-terminals ambalwa asuselwa ku-windows afana neWindows Terminal, i-fluent terminal, wsltty, conemu, kepha ekugcineni ngishintshele ku wezterm . Yize iyisiginali yakamuva inazo zonke izici ongazifuna kusuka ku-emulator yesiginali:

  • amathebhu nama-panes, ngendlela yokuthi awudingi i-tmux

  • i-multiplexing (imodi ekude engenakhanda), yokuthi awudingi i-tmux

  • imodi yokukopisha umbhalo ene-vi keybindings, yokuthi awudingi i-tmux

  • ukukhetha okusheshayo, imodi yokusikisela ukukhetha izinto zombhalo ngokushesha ukusuka emuva-emuva

  • imiyalo ezenzakalelayo yamathebhu amasha

  • Ukwenza ngokwezifiso okuhle kakhulu kwamamephu wokhiye, ngaphandle kweqiniso lokuthi awukwazi ukusebenzisa i-Left / Right Shift / Ctrl / Alt njengezinguquko ezihlukile.

Nge-Windows ngibuye ngashintsha ukuhamba kwami ​​kokusebenza kancane kangangoba angidingi i-terminal enemodi ye-daemon esekela ama-windows ahlukene amaningi, ngokusebenzisa ukuphela okwehlayo. Ukwenza wezterm Okwehlayo ngemuva kokuzama imibhalo eminingi ye-AHK eyehlayo, ekugcineni ngathola eyodwa lokho kusebenze kahle. Enye indlela engcono kungaba windows-ukuphela-ukuzamazama komhlaba, kepha ngibe nenkinga ngokungaziphathi kahle ngamadeskithophu angokoqobo aphethwe yi-dexpot, kanti i-mitty-quake-console ibonakala ihambisana namadeski we-dexpot. Cishe lapho isipiliyoni se-Windows virtual desktops sithuthuka, ngizoshintshela ku-windows-terminal-quake. I-WezTerm futhi i-cross-platform okusho ukuthi, ngingaqhubeka ngiyisebenzise ngokufakwa kwendabuko kwe-linux. Kube ukutholakala ngenhlanhla ngempela kwalokhu kushintshwa kweWindows + WSL, ngoba uKitty akakasekeli iWindows, nezinye izindlela zokugcina, kube nezicasulo ezinkulu kakhulu Angikwazanga ukwamukela.
