Ukuthuthukisa imbobo kanogwaja ku-python

Ukusheshisa ukubuyiselwa emuva kwe-freqtrade crypto trading bot.

I-Freqtrade Ingabe i-crypto trading bot ku-python. Ngangivame ukudlala ngemfologo isikhashana, ngilungisa iziphazamisi nokuziphatha engangingakuthandi. Ukufaka ohlwini izimbungulu lapha kungaba yisicefe ngakho-ke ngizokhuluma ngezinto engicabanga ukuthi ziyathakazelisa.

Isebenza kanjani i-freqtrade (FQT), ezingeni eliphezulu

I-FQT ayisekelwe emcimbini, isekelwe ku-loop. Kusho ukuthini lokhu? Singase sikhuluma ngomenzi wefa (ukusebenza bukhoma) noma i-backtester. Kulokhu zombili zisekelwe kwi-loop.

Indlela emfushane lapha. Ngikhetha ini? Amaluphu noma Imicimbi?

  • Amaluphu ngokuvamile alula futhi kulula ukubonisana nawo, uma kuqhathaniswa nemicimbi. Imicimbi ye-OTOH ingamodela umhlaba wangempela kangcono. Amaluphu nawo kulula ukuwaqhathanisa uma kuqhathaniswa nemicimbi, futhi enza ngokujwayelekile ngokushesha.

  • Ukuhlolwa kwe-Loop okusekelwe ku-Loop kungaqhutshwa eceleni nomanzi bukhoma. Lokhu kusho ukuthi ukuhlola okubuyela emuva kuba yingxenye yesu lakho lokuhweba. Ukusebenzisa isistimu yokuhlola i-backtesting esekelwe kumcimbi phila kubonakala sengathi isiphithiphithi kimi. Lokho kungenxa yokuthi imicimbi idinga umbono wesikhathi, kodwa ngesikhathi sokuhweba bukhoma unesikhathi samanje kuphela, ngakho-ke yini ongayilingisa noma kanjani futhi kanjani?

...ibuyela ku-FQT, kumodi ebukhoma, icubungula ama-oda ku-loop. Njalo ngemizuzwana engu-X ibuza ukushintshaniswa kwezibuyekezo. Igcina amathrekhi alandelayo:

  • uhlu lwababili oluphethe zonke izintengo zokugcina zazo zonke izimpahla (zokushintshana okusekelayo)

  • idatha entsha ye-OHLCV

  • isimo sama-oda avulekile

Ruh-roh

Ukubulawa bukhoma okusekelwe ku-Loop kunenkinga esobala. Iyahambisana. Uma ucubungula kumapheya amaningi ukwenza ngeke kusabele kancane, futhi kuphindaphindeke kaningi, kusale ngemuva kokwembuleka kwesenzo senani. Ngakho-ke naphezu kweqiniso lokuthi ngikhetha izihibe zokuhlehla emuva, ngincamela imicimbi yokubulawa bukhoma.

Ububi? Uma usebenzisa imicimbi entweni eyodwa bese usebenzisa enye, kulula ukuthi amamodeli noma amapharamitha esu ahluke futhi angafani neqiniso.

I-agumenti yami yokuzivikela ngokumelene nalokhu ukuthi ukuhlale ngizihlola (ukubuyisela emuva) nokwenza bukhoma kuvimbela le nkinga ngoba ekugcineni qondanisa.

I-FQT inamapharamitha amaningi wokucupha futhi izikhathi eziningi umehluko phakathi kwe-bot nesu uzoba mpunga njengoba isu lingakwazi ukulawula ukuthi ama-oda asetshenziswa kanjani i-bot. I-bot iphatha i-"roi" okuyigridi yemikhawulo yentengo yokwenza inzuzo kanye "ne-trailing stoploss" ngokufanayo kodwa ohlangothini oluphansi. Isu lihlinzeka ngamapharamitha kuphela futhi i-bot yenza okunye. Ngangingeyena umlandeli walokhu.

Izingcingo zokuthenga nokudayisa amasignali. Ukuhweba kuzokwenzeka ngokusekelwe kumasiginali abuyiswe ama-callbacks azobala isignali kuhlaka lwedatha. Inkinga ngalokhu ukuthi isignali imile; ungavele ubale ngokuguqukayo uhlaka lwedatha oluwumugqa ongu-1, futhi ukuphakele lokho ku-bot, kodwa ngeke kuphenduleke. Lokhu kuhle uma ungakuthandi ukubuyisela emuva okungako, komunye nomunye.

Ama-patchworks

Engikulungise ku-FQT ukuze ngizame ukuyenza iziphathe ngendlela engiyithanda ngayo.

  • Izihlungi zohlu olubili . Ngokunciphisa inani lamapheya azocutshungulwa ekuphindaphindweni ngakunye, unganciphisa umthwalo ngezindleko zokuphinda kusebenze okuthile. Ubusazocubungula zonke izimpahla obekunokuthengiswa kwazo okuvulelekile, kanye nenani elilinganiselwe lezintsha. Ngakho ngakha

    • A ukushova isihlungi ukukhetha nje amapheya ngokungahleliwe.

    • Isihlungi se-robin esiyindilinga ukuze siphindaphinde ngamapheya kancane kancane.

    • Uhlu olumile, oluzolayishwa lusuka kusitoreji

  • Amafomethi wedatha: Ngengeze ukwesekwa kwe-hdf, i-parquet futhi zarr . Kulokhu ngizithole ngisebenzisa i-zarr, ngenxa ye-buildin yayo esheshayo ye-de/compression of data. Ilula kune-parquet futhi i-ergonomic ngaphezulu.

Kukhona inqwaba yamanye ama-tweaks, njengokuhlola amasiginali afanayo engigcine ngiphumile njengoba ngigxile kakhulu ekwakheni amasiginali asheshayo, nama-tweaks wokuhlela nokuhlela izinketho, kepha awaphawuleki.

Ukufuna i-vectorized i-backtest

Ngizamile izinguqulo eziningi ze-backtesting ukuze ngisheshise. Kwakufanele konke, neze. I-Computing logic phakathi komsebenzi wokuthenga nokuthengisa usebenzisa ama-numpy arrays. Kufana nokudlala i-tetris ngobuchopho bakho. Ngiqonde lapho amabhlogo enziwa khona nge-grey matter yakho, futhi uzama ukuhlanganisa. Lapha sizibala

  • I-backtest ehlanganisiwe . Kulokhu sizozama ukudiliza ukuhweba sisebenzisa ama-numpy array kuphela. Ukujula phakathi kokugeleza okulungile kokusetshenziswa kokuthenga/ukuthengisa, amasignali, izitophu, izitophusi ezilandelanayo, ukubala kwegridi ye-roi, izibalo ze-roi ezinesisindo sesikhathi. Konke okunezinhlaka ezincane, bekuyinhlakanhlaka impela.

  • Isekelwe ku-VBT Le nguqulo yazama ukusebenzisa amandla i-vectorbtnumba based python library ukuze wenze i-backtest. Ngokumangazayo kwakungasheshi kakhulu kunenguqulo ye-numpy. Kwakungenxa yokuthi kwakufanele kube nezinhlobo eziningi zokuguqulwa zibe izinhlobo ezihambisanayo ze-VBT, ngakho-ke noma isiphi isivinini esizuzwe ekubulaweni salahleka lapho.

  • Ukweqa phezu kobubanzi Lona bekuwumzamo wokuqala wokusebenzisa i-numba ngokwami. Ngoba i-numba ngokwemvelo ingabiza imisebenzi engu-numpy, ngiguqule uhlaka lwami lwe-backtest olusekelwe ku-numpy ukuze lusebenze ngaphansi kwe-numba. Inzuzo yayincane kakhulu. Izindleko zokubeka yonke into ku-numpy array kwaba ibhodlela. Izinzuzo ezizuzwe kamuva bezincane njengoba ngemva kokuguqulwa kwe-numpy, ukubulawa kwe-numpy sekuvele kushesha.

  • Ukuvula amakhandlela Lokhu kwaba futhi kufanele kube ngeyokugcina. Kwaba ukuqaliswa okwenza kahle kakhulu. Lokho kungenxa yokuthi bekungukuphindaphinda "okulula" phezu kwamakhandlela, konke kwenziwa ngaphakathi kwemisebenzi ye-numba jitted. Umqondo wawulula, kodwa ukusebenzisa yonke i-roi, kanye ne-stoploss logic ngenkathi usebenzisana nezimbungulu ze-numba kwakungekuhle. Kwasebenza kahle ekugcineni, kodwa kwakungemva kwentaba yokungahambelani kwe-numba okwadingeka ngikhuphuke.

Singeze nezinye izici ezengeziwe ku-backtest, njengokuthi ukusabalala kanye ne-liquidity ukubala, kanye nesikhathi esishiwo ngaphambili esikaliniwe roi.

Ukufuna ngokuhambisana ukwenza kahle

Kungani bengifuna i-backtester esheshayo? Ngoba ngifuna ukusebenzisa eziningi zazo ukuze ngikwazi ukuthola ukulungiselelwa okungcono kakhulu kwepharamitha...yebo, ngokunganaki noma yimuphi umqondo wokulingana ngokweqile, ukulinganisa ngokweqile, ukuntuleka kokugxila, njll... Ake sibhale engisebenze kukho:

  • Ukuthumela imisebenzi kwabanye abasebenzi (izinqubo) ngokushesha ngangokunokwenzeka. Ukuthuthukiswa kokuqala kwaba ukushintsha indlela okuthunyelwa ngayo imisebenzi. Kusukela ku-Dispatch N imisebenzi futhi ngilinde ukuthi yonke iphele, ngishintshe inqubo ukuze ngiphakele imisebenzi emisha ngokushesha nje lapho eyedlule isiqediwe. Kube nenkinga ngale ndlela nokho. Ukuthuthukisa kulandelana izikhathi eziningi. Ngeke wazi ukuthi imaphi amaphoyinti esikhala sepharamitha okufanele uwabhekisise, ngaphandle kokuthi uqedele ukwenza ikhompuyutha edlule. Lokhu kuyiqiniso okungenani ngokulungiselelwa kwe-Bayesian. Lokhu kusho ukuthi uma sisampula izivivinyo ezintsha ngokuqhubekayo ngeke sithuthukise usesho kakhulu phakathi kokuthuthukisa, kodwa ukusesha kuzoba namandla kakhulu.

    Singasebenzisa esinye isilungiseleli esingasuselwe ku-bayesian optimization, njenge Lena . Noma sebenzisa izilungiseleli ezizimele ze-N, njengokuthi inqubo/i-optimizer ngayinye ibuze imisebenzi emisha isebenzisa umlando wayo, noma ngokuzikhethela umlando owabiwe nabanye (yize lokhu kungenza zonke izithuthukisi zihlangane ngemuva kwesikhathi esithile, obekungaba kuhle. impahla, noma cha, kuye ngecala).

    Ukwabelana ngemibono phakathi kwazo zonke izinqubo kwaba inhlekelele ngokuphelele. Ukukhetha nokukhipha izinhlu ezinkulu zokuntanta kuhamba kancane. Njengoba ukusesha kuqhubeka, inani lokubhekwa liyakhula, futhi yonke inqubo ihamba kancane futhi ihamba kancane kuze kube seqophelweni lapho ukuhlelwa kwe-serial kuthatha isikhathi esiningi kunokuhlola ukuhlola. Ibihamba kancane kokubili kusetshenziswa inqubo yomphathi, amafayela angenalutho, noma afakwe imephu ye-mem. Okuncono bhadla imali ngakho-ke i-approach iphenduke ukusebenzisa izilungiseleli ezihlukene futhi ungalokothi wabelane ngokukubonile phakathi kwazo, gcina yonke into enkumbulweni.

  • Ukulandelela isimo sokuthuthukisa. Cabanga usebenzisa ukulungiselelwa amahora, bese okuthile kuphahlazeka bese ukhulula yonke into. Yebo besifuna ukukugwema lokho, ngakho-ke sengeze okunengqondo kukho yonga ngezikhathi ezithile . Lona bekuwumshayeli oyinhloko wokwengeza ukwesekwa kwesitoreji se-zarr, ukonga isimo nge-zarr kuthuthukise izinto kakhulu.

  • Ukubuka ngeso lokuthuthukisa okwenziwe. I-FQT isivele ibe nokubonwayo okuhle kokusebenza nokwenza kahle. Ngithuthuke kukho. Kwadingeka ngidale i into ukumela konke ukubonwa. Bese ngikwazi ukuqalisa ukucutshungulwa kokuthunyelwe okwengeziwe njenge ukujwayela futhi ukuhlunga

  • Ngoba Ukuqinisekiswa Kwesiphambano kufanele sihlukanise idatha, futhi sisebenzise i-backtesting phezu kobubanzi obuhlukahlukene. Ngenxa yemizamo yethu yokulandelela isimo sokuthuthukisa, singasebenzisa okukhiphayo okuvela ekuqinisekisweni okuphambene njengembewu kwenye inqubo yokuthuthukisa ecwengisiswe kakhudlwana...ukugcwala ngokweqile...kunjalo.

  • I-logic eyengeziwe yokuthuthukisa. Ukuze uzuze ubuningi qhuqha kwadingeka futhi siphendule ngezinkinga ezithile:

    • Kwesinye isikhathi ukulungiselelwa kwepharamitha kungase kumane kwehluleke, futhi isilungiseleli singase sibambeke, ngakho-ke bekufanele silandele umkhondo wokuqaphelile okuhlulekile, futhi ekugcineni siqale kabusha okukodwa, noma zonke izilungiseleli.

    • Ngenxa yokuthi besisebenzisa izilungiseleli eziningi, bekufanele silandelele ukuthi yisiphi isithuthukisi esikhiqize ukubonwa.

    • Sibonga i-backtester yethu esheshayo, kanye nesikhuthazi sethu esifanayo, besihlola okuningi, i-FQT izophrinta ukubonwa ngakunye njengoba iqeda. Kwakudingeka sihlanganise esikubonile ngaphambi kokukuphrinta, ukuze siphrinte a"tableful" kuzo esikhundleni sokubheka okukodwa ngesikhathi, njengoba ukusebenza kwe-IO kuyabiza.

    • Ukuhlola/ukuxhashazwaibhalansi. Okunye ukugijima kungase kubambeke ku-minima, noma kuhlangane ukuze kancane, ngakho-ke sengeze amandla okushintsha isikhala sokusesha endizeni . Lokhu kuzosiza ukwenza ijubane lokusesha libe ngokwejwayelekile.

    • Ibha yokuqhubeka engcono. Izilungiseleli ezisebenzayo ezinqubweni eziningi zidinga ukuvumelanisa ukuze kubuyekezwe ibha yenqubekelaphambili kubasebenzi abaningi. Futhi kwakukhona umtapo wezincwadi nje lokho , okwasivumela ukuthi futhi sengeze ulwazi olwengeziwe esimweni sokusebenza sokuthuthukisa.

    • Ngenxa yokuthi besisebenzisa izilungiseleli eziningi, siphinde sengeza usekelo ukuze sinikeze imisebenzi ehlukene yokulahlekelwa kuzilungiseleli ezihlukene, okungasivumela ukuthi sibone ukuthi yimuphi umsebenzi wokulahlekelwa owenze kahle kakhulu.

  • Ukuze siqalise ukulungiselelwa, sisebenzisa i-a cli iskripthi ukusebenzisa ukulungiselelwa ngamapharamitha ahlukene wokucupha, qalisa kabusha kusukela kusimo sangaphambilini, noma vele ubonise futhi uhlunge izivivinyo ezilondoloziwe.

  • Besisebenzisa izilungiseleli ezizimele ezinemisebenzi ehlukene yokulahlekelwa, kungani-ke ungasebenzisi ama-optimizers ahlukahlukene vula imisebenzi ehlukene yokulahlekelwa ? Ingqondo ishaywa. Ngakho sisebenzise i- ukukhipha kuzilungiseleli ezifana nokuthi sikwazi (ngokufaka amapayipi kancane) ngokumangalisayo ukushintsha indlela yokwenza. Lokhu kuphinde kwadinga ukulungisa ukulungiselelwa esibonakalayo ukuze "i-hyperopt" ikwazi ukuqonda i-optimizer. Sabe sesiqalisa kabusha scikit-optimize njengesibonelo sokukhishwa kwethu kwe-optimizer. Ngenkathi ixhuma futhi imbazo, emukit futhi i-sherpa.

Iziphetho

Sifake i-backtesting ye-FQT kuye 11 . Kodwa asikaze sikusebenzise ngempela ekukhiqizeni :).

Kancane emva kwe ukuba ndikindikiwe-backtester, ama-callbacks angeziwe angeziwe esu eliphule ukuhlukana phakathi kokuhlaziya i-backtesting kanye nokuhlolwa kwamasu, okusho ukuthi ukugcina into ishesha, kwakudingeka futhi ubhale isu lakho nge-numba! Kepha ngicikekile ukuxutshwa okuxegayo kwe-python/numpy/numba gotchas futhi ngenxa yokuthi angikuthandanga ukubulawa bukhoma kwe-FQT, ngilahle yonke into noma kunjalo, ngokuluhlaza (noma ngizothi pinker! ) amadlelo.

Noma kunjalo...ukwenza kahle kuyingozi.
