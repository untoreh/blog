Indlela yokutshala iminqumo

Ungayitshala kanjani iminqumo, umhlahlandlela omfishane futhi ophelele

Izihlahla ezingaphansi kweminyaka engu- ~ 3

Ukutshala

  • Lapho utshala iminqumo, kuyalulekwa ukuthi ubeke i- umbhede wamatshe ukuvumela amanzi ageleze amanzi, lokhu akudingeki ezindaweni ezifudumele, futhi mhlawumbe kuphikisane nokukhiqiza ngoba kuzothikameza imisebenzi izinkuni chirurgy lapho izihlahla zikhula.

  • A induku isetshenziselwa ukuphoqa isihlahla ukuthi sikhule sibheke phezulu ezigabeni zaso zokuqala, induku kufanele ibe yizindawo kuisiqondiso esiphambene kwe umoya onamandla kunawo wonke kwendawo, ukuphikisa ukwenza okungaguquguquki ukugoba inqubo esetshenziswa yimimoya isiqu esikhulu.

  • Akufanele kwenziwe ukuthenwa, futhi uhlelo malukhule ngokwemvelo yakhe ukuma ehlathini.

  • Izimpande esigabeni esincane zikhula njenge izimpande , ujulile emhlabathini futhi mfushane ezinhlangothini. Lokhu ikakhulukazi kungumphumela odalwa ukwakheka kokuqala okunikezwe izitshalo ezikhuliswe enkulisa yezitshalo. Izitshalo ezihlume endle kufanele ziqale ukukhula izimpande ezithandekayo zisuka. Izimpande zimbi ngoba ngemuva kwesigaba esincanyana, ngemuva kokuba izimpande ezithandekayo zigcine sezithathile, ziqala ukubola, zishiya isitshalo sisengozini yokushona ngaphansi komhlaba isikhunta sihlasela.

  • Izitshalo ezincane zinjalo abasengozini enkulu kwisomiso.

Izihlahla ezingaphansi kweminyaka engu- ~ 10

  • Sekuyisikhathi sokukhetha amagatsha amakhulu azokwehlukanisa isiqu ku-2 bese kutholakala oku-4.

  • Qiniseka ukuthi amagatsha akhethiwe axosha ngaphandle futhi ngalinye emakhoneni ahlukene wesikwele sesigaba.

  • Ukuhlukaniswa kokuqala kufanele kube cishe ngama-80cm-1m ukusuka emhlabathini.

  • Ukuthambekela kwabo kufanele kube ngu-30/60 Â°.

  • Qiniseka ukuthi amagatsha akhethiwe awahambi kahle futhi anamagatsha amancane abalekela phezulu.

Ezinye izindlela zokuthena

Le esanda kuchazwa iyindlela ejwayelekile yokuthena izihlahla zeminqumo e okungezona ezeqile emasimini. Ezinye izindlela ezinjenge- epsylon futhi isihlahla sikakhisimusi azibonisanga izinzuzo ngakho-ke azithandeki.

Okuwukuphela kokunye okuvumelekile kokuma kwe-polyconic yi- imbulungashape, yize isimo se-polyconic singashintshwa ukuze sanelise izakhiwo zesimo sembulunga ngokukhulisa idome elincanyana lamagatsha amancane aphuma ngaphakathi esiqongweni segatsha eliphakeme kakhulu.

Ngemuva kweminyaka engu- ~ 10

Lesi yisikhathi lapho izihlahla ziqala khona ukukhiqiza isivuno esihle. Ukuthena kulesi sigaba kuqukethe

Ukuncipha kombhede

  • sika amagatsha aphelelwe amandla, okuyilawa magatsha asondele kakhulu kunsisekelo yokuxhuma kwegatsha (egatsheni elikhulu), futhi amadala kunamanye amagatsha (eduze kwamathiphu). Lawo magatsha (akhiqize iminqumo eminyakeni eyedlule), ambozwe amagatsha amancane namancane aseduze namathiphu, manje anciphise ukugeleza kwempuphu futhi azoma kancane njengoba isihlahla siqondisa kabusha ubisi lapho amagatsha gaya ama-carbs amaningi sibonga ukuvezwa kokukhanya okuphezulu.

  • usike amahlumela, asondele kakhulu ekuhlukanisweni kwegatsha; Njengoba indawo lapho kwenzeka khona ukuhlukaniswa kabili kuvame ukuba nendawo evundlile, amagatsha aqhamuka emgodini wegatsha avame ukukhula ngokuma phezulu lapho efuna ukukhanya okungenakugwenywa.

  • Ungazisiki zonke, qala esisekelweni bese usika u-2 noma u-3 (yize kuya ngosayizi wamagatsha) bese ushiya konke okunye, okuzoba yingxenye ekhiqizayo yegatsha eminyakeni ezayo.

  • usike amagatsha amade kakhulu, futhi abaleke ukwakheka kombhede.

  • kepha futhi nalabo abangaphakathi kakhulu, ngoba uhlala ufuna ukwakha igatsha lokukhiqiza njengeV , ngenhloso ekuyo yonke indawo yokwandisa ukuhamba komoya nokukhanya okulula.

  • usike wonke amahlumela aqhamuka emagatsheni amakhulu bese ekhula aye phezulu noma ngaphakathi (ngasenkabeni), uqiniseke ukuthi ushiya lawo akhethiwe ukuthi athathe indawo yamagatsha amakhulu ngokwawo (okufanele abheke phezulu noma ngaphandle kancane).

  • usike amagatsha amaningi agulayo ngangokunokwenzeka (njengamagatsha athinteka yi ifindo lomnqumo ), kepha ngiyaqiniseka ukuthi ngihamba njalo okuthile . Imvamisa ukuthena akufanele kususe okungaphezulu kwe-1/3 kwetende, kepha ezimweni ezimbi kakhulu kwamukelekile ukufinyelela2/3.

Ukushintsha amagatsha amakhulu

  • ngokukhulisa izinto ezingangena eduze nendawo emaphakathi kwesihlahla esivela emagatsheni azothathelwa indawo okuyi:

    • ogulayo, obuthakathaka (uhlaza oluphansi) noma owomile (isisulu sezimpande ezibolayo)

    • kusinda kakhulu (ngendlela yokuthi igatsha elithambekele limboze amagatsha angomakhelwane)

  • Abazobambela kufanele baboshwe (ngentambo noma okulingana) negatsha elizosikwa, ngenhloso yokuqondisa igatsha elincane liqonde kwelidala.

  • Amagatsha amila kanjani amazinyane? Ithiphu yegatsha ngalinye iqhuma njalo amagugu amabili ngasikhathi (ezinhlangothini eziphambene), nge kushintshana 180Â° orientation at each generation. One of the branches is sterile and goes upwards, where as the fertile one tend to be pendulous. This is not an accurate definition but can help choosing which isimo ukunikeza umuthi. Igatsha ngalinye ekugcineni lizothela ngokuhluma amagatsha amasha kuzeluleko zawo. Ukuhluma kwenzeka entwasahlobo nasekwindla, okuvame ukuba yizikhathi ezinomswakama ophakeme, futhi kuvame ukuhambisana nezikhathi zokuthena, okwenza ukukhetha kwesikhathi sokuthena nakho kubalulekile.

    • Ngasiphi isikhathi sonyaka isihlahla kufanele sithenwe? Izihlahla ze-olive ziyancipha ebusika nasehlobo (ngenxa yesomiso), okwenza lezi kube yizikhathi ezifanele zokuthena. Kodwa-ke emagatsheni athena izithelo athela izithelo akulungile noma ngabe akulimazi isihlahla uqobo. Njengoba izitshalo ziphula ukulala ngemuva kuka ~ 11 Â° Isikhathi esifanele sokuthena (noma okungenani ukusika okunqume kakhulu) kusekupheleni kobusika (njengokuthi amanxeba avulekile angathintwa yiqhwa elibi), ekuqaleni kwentwasahlobo (ngaphambi kokuba umuthi uqale khetha ukuthi zingaki izinsizakusebenza ozozibekela i-fructification).

Ukuphakama

  • ukusika iziqongo eziphakeme kakhulu (shiya njalo onqenqemeni)

    • Ukuphakama okuphezulu kwesihlahla kuncike ebude besitebhisi osisebenzisela ukuthena (uma usebenzisa iladi), noma ubude bamathuluzi akho we-telescopic (uma ungasebenzisi iladi.)

Isikhunta

  • bamba izinkuni ezibolile ezibangelwa isikhunta sihlasela esisekelweni noma emagatsheni ane-chainsaw noma i-hatchet.

Ungakusika kanjani ukusika

Uma uthena, noma yikuphi ukukhetha kubila ekwenzeni ukusikeka, indlela owenza ngayo kuthinte umuthi, futhi njengoba ungalinganisa ukusikeka kwamakhulu esihlahleni ngasinye, konke kuyahlangana. Le mithetho isebenza kunoma yisiphi isihlahla ngokujwayelekile, hhayi izihlahla zeminqumo kuphela.

  • Ukusikeka okukhulu, lapho le mithetho ithwala isisindo esikhulu, njengomthetho wesithupha:

    • ngaphansi kwe1cm azibalulekile

    • Ngaphezu kwe2cm izinto ziqala ukuba nendaba

    • Khumbula ukuthi indawo ingumsebenzi we-quadratic, okusho ukuthi imiphumela yokwanda kokwehluka endaweni futhi ikhule njengamandla amabili.

  • Kusala malini? Ukushiya imiphumela eningi kwifayela le- isiphunzi. izimbobo kumagxolo (izimbobo zimbi, zishiya ukugeleza kwangaphakathi kobisi kuvezwe kakhulu kokungaphandle, futhi kuvumela amanzi ukuma , ukwakha indawo ekahle yesikhunta esiphikisayo esiphikisayo).

    • Njengoba ukusikeka okuningi kuzobandakanya ukusika amaswidi, aneminyaka yobudala eyi-1-3 (noma ngaphezulu ngokuvamile iminyaka engaphansi noma elingana nobuningi bokuthena), kulula ukuhlukanisa omncane amagxolo amahlumela, avela amadala elinye lamagatsha amakhulu, ukusika kufanele kwenziwe kulo mngcele, ukuze kuthi wonke amagxolo amancane esihlumela asuswe, futhi kungabikho khonkolo elidala elikhishwayo.

    • Uma amagatsha amadala, nomehluko wobudala bamagxolo akulula ukuwubona (emngceleni bobabili babukeka begugile), omunye umkhondo osiza ukusikwa kungumumo wegatsha ozonqunywa. Imvamisa umngcele ophakathi kwamagatsha awuyona i-angle eqinile, kunalokho ufana nejika elihle. Uma kubhekwa isigaba esime mpo, ukusika kuzokwenziwa lapho imigqa emibili efanayo yegatsha izowela ngaphakathi igxolo.

    • Ukuqondiswa kokusika ngokujwayelekile kufanele kube i-orthogonalekuqondeni lapho igatsha elizosikwa likhula khona. Ukwenza ukusikeka ngale ndlela kuqinisekisa ukuthi bancane ngangokunokwenzeka ngoba (uma kubhekwa isigaba esivundlile) ukusika kusondele kumbuthano futhi kuqhubeke kusuka kusiyingi, njengoba kunikezwe ububanzi besiyingi, futhi kucatshangwa ukuthi ubukhulu obuncane be-ellipse ngokufana nombuthano, indawo ye-ellipse izohlala ingaphezulu noma ilingana nendawo yombuthano. Sekukonke, kusho ukuthi ukusikeka okuncane kuholela emanxebeni amancane, okuholela ekuphulukisweni ngokushesha nasekungeneni kwezifo.

      • Kukhona okuhlukile nokho, lapho ukusikeka kuzogcina kubheke phezulu, kungcono ukubenza bathambe kancane; lokhu kuvumela imvula ukuba yehle ngokungathi ibiyovela ophahleni, esikhundleni sokumba izimbobo ngokungenakuphikiswa enxebeni elishiywe ukusika.

    • Amagatsha amancane anemicu eminingi, amadala anemisipha eminingi. Kusho ukuthini lokhu ukuthi okusele okuvela emagatsheni amancane kuzokwenza cishe oma. Njengoba imicu igcwele kakhulu, isengozini enkulu yomoya nelanga uma kuqhathaniswa namagatsha amadala amakhulu. Futhi amagxolo akhudlwana amagatsha amadala avimbela umswakama ekuphunyukeni.

  • Hlanganisa i-engela elilodwa (le-4) ngasikhathi, uqale phezulu bese wehlela ngasisekelo.

    • Ngaphandle kokuthi uhlela ukusika amagatsha aphakeme akhule abheke phansi kakhulu, lapho-ke uqala kulawo, bese eqhubeka njengokujwayelekile, phezulu kuye ezansi (yize ukusikeka okungaxakeki kangako ngoba usanda kusika kakhulu phansi.)

Umhlabathi, Umanyolo, amanzi nezithelo

  • Izihlahla zomnqumo zidinga initrogen eningi, ngoba kufanele zishintshe amahlamvu azo njalo. (Ukuhlukaniswa okuningiliziwe kwawo wonke amaminerali bheka leli phepha)

  • Njengasemasimini amaningi ufuna ukuxubana nomanyolo oncibilikayo nophilayo

  • Amanzi abalulekile entwasahlobo, ngaphambi nangesikhathi sokuqhakaza kwezimbali, ukusiza ukubumbana; ukungakunaki ukwenza kanjalo entwasahlobo eyomile, kuzodala ukuthi izithelo eziningi ezincane ziwe ngaphambi kokufika ehlobo.

  • Amanzi nawo abalulekile ngesikhathi sasehlobo, hhayi ngekhwalithi yezithelo, kepha kusabalulekile esitshalweni nesikhathi sokuvuna. Njengoba indlela yemishini yokuvuna iminqumo kungukuzamazama komuthi, isihlahla esibuthakathaka esingaphansi kwengcindezi enjalo sizokhulula futhi amahlamvu aso amaningi kakhulu, ngakho-ke kubalulekile ukuthi uqiniseke ukuthi izihlahla zinombhede oqinile ngaphambi kokuvuna. Ukunisela kwehlobo nakho kwenza izithelo zikhule zizungeze (futhi zikhudlwana, yize usayizi weminqumo uncike ezinhlotsheni ezilinyiwe)

  • Abanye abantu bathi ukuchelela izihlahla zomnqumo ehlobo kuholela ekukhiqizweni okuphezulu kweminqumo, kepha hhayi isivuno esiphezulu samafutha omnqumo futhi kwenza ukukhishwa kukawoyela kube nzima (njengoba imishini kufanele ihlukanise amanzi amaningi nakoyela). Ngamanye amagama, njengoba izihlahla zomnqumo "ziziphilisa", i-trichoderma ne-mycorrhizae kungathathwa njengokuphulukisa izithuthukisi.

  • Ukulima, ukulima noma utshani? Kuya ngohlobo lomhlabathi nesimo sezulu esimaphakathi, noma kunjalo:

    • ngoba izimpande zomuthi womnqumo azivame ukukhula zijule ngaphezu kwemitha eli-1, kulinywa ukutshalwa. Izihlahla zeminqumo zivame ukukhula futhi kunamadwalainhlabathi, lapho ngisho nokulima kungenzeka, futhi ukusebenza kwenhlabathi kuncishiselwa ekugundweni. Ukulima nokulima kusebenza kangcono uma usebenzisa ama-herbicides ukulawula ukhula, kanti ngaphandle kokugunda ukhula kusiza ekulondolozeni imvelo yamagciwane enhlabathini. Lapho ukhetha ukugunda, ukulawula uhlobo lwamakhambi asele kungaba lusizo, njengesibonelo ukukhetha utshani obumelana nesomiso. Inzuzo enkulu yokulima / yokulima i-oxygenation ejulile, ngakolunye uhlangothi utshani busiza ekuqedeni ukuguguleka kwenhlabathi, ukubola kwezakhamzimba zenhlabathi, isomiso (ngokugcinwa kwamanzi okuphezulu). Kuyinsakavukela ukumboza inhlabathi ngo-80% ngokwephula, ezimweni lapho kufakwa kuphela ukulima ngaphezulu.

Isiphetho

Izihlahla zomnqumo ziyizitshalo ezinamandla kakhulu, kodwa zinjalo abafuna ukukhanya . Kukonke kulula ukuthi kutshalwe iminqumo lapho kunelanga eliningi khona namanzi anele. Izitshalo zinezindlela zemvelo zokunqanda izifo, futhi zidinga ukunakekelwa ngokumelene nezifo ezinkulu ezi-3 kuphela ( amabala, ukubola futhi ukundiza ), ngenkathi konke okunye kungabhekwa njengokuncane. Kepha ayizithandi izimo zezulu ezinzima, ayibekezeleli amazinga okushisa angaphansi kuka-0 Â° nangaphezulu kuka-30 Â°, izimo zezulu ezimanzi kakhulu nezimo ezomile kakhulu. Yize izihlahla zomnqumo ezikhulayo zingenzeka ezindaweni ezahlukahlukene, ziyasebenza kuphela kwezomnotho ezifundeni ezingaphansi komhlaba.

iqiniso elimnandi

Kwakucatshangwa ukuthi kuyakhula okusanhlamvu phakathi kwezihlahla zomnqumo kwakukubi ngoba ukukhanya okukhombisa izakhiwo zezindlebe kungacindezela izihlahla zomnqumo ngakho-ke imidumba babekhethwa. Imidumba empeleni ikhiqiza okuningi i-nitrogen , iziphi izihlahla zeminqumo ezikulangazelelayo (ngokwesilinganiso ngaphezu kwezinye izitshalo).
