Ewayinini le-EU akulona ncamashi amagilebhisi kepha ujusi ubilile kusuka vitis vinifera . Okungafaki i- [ vitis labrusca ], amagilebhisi aseMelika avela. Okufanele kubizwe ngokuthile okufana nofana nolunye ujusi ovutshiwe ongenziwanga ngalo i-vitis vinifer.

Lokhu kuzolunga ... Uma ufuna ukubiza iwayini kuphela ngezinto ezenziwe ngesitshalo esithile. Ngaphandle kokuthi amazwe ahlukene anemithetho ehlukile. E-Italy awuvunyelwe ukufaka amanzi, noma ushukela ngesikhathi sokuvutshelwa, kwamanye amazwe kodwa ukuxubana komthetho, futhi okugcina kubizwa ngokuthi iwayini kungahluka kakhulu kuye ngezwe.

Ibizwa iwayini , kepha ngenqubo yokuvutshelwa ebukeka ifana kakhulu ubhiya ... ngaphandle kwalelo qiniso lokuthi usebenzisa okusanhlamvu hhayi izithelo (zomvini). Ukuziba iqiniso lokuthi lapho kwenziwa iwayini, uhlu lwe izithakoezisetshenziselwa ukuzinzisa izakhiwo ezinjenge-tannins, umbala, ukucaca, i-asidi nokunciphisa ukuvutshelwa isikhathi eside kakhulu, ukusabalalisa umkhiqizo wokugcina ngisho nangaphezulu kwalokho okufanele kube ijusi elifakiwe kusuka ku-vitis vinifera.

Kulesi sigaba esingenangqondo salokho okubizwa yiYurophu iwayini kufanele sicacise ukuthi ukwehlukanisa phakathi kwewayini elikhiqizwe ngaphandle koshukela noma amanzi angeziwe kusuka umsulwa iwayini alinakwenzeka, alikho izigxivizo zeminwe ehlukanisa okumsulwa kokufakelwayo, ungasebenzisa i-heuristic ukuqhathanisa okuqukethwe kwezinye izinto ezijwayelekile ku- umsulwa iwayini, ngaphandle kwalelo qiniso lokuthi ukubalwa okungenakubalwa kokuhlukahluka phakathi kwama-cultivars, iminyaka yokuvuna nezindawo, kwenza ukwakhiwa kwephrofayili yewayini yasekuqaleni kusondele kakhulu kungenzeki.

Uyini umphumela? Amanye amazwe anokuthunyelwa kwewayini okunamandla amakhulu ngoba umthetho wawo uvumela inkululeko ethe xaxa ukumboza izingxenye ezahlukahlukene zemakethe, futhi ungaguqula kangcono ubuningi bokukhiqiza ngokuvumelana nesidingo.

Kulokhu, engeza iqiniso lokuthi EFrance ngokwesibonelo, uye wavumela kusuka eminyakeni embalwa eyedlule abalimi ukuthi batshale izivini ngenhloso yokuhweba noma kuphi, kanti I-Italy isendleleni ehamba kancane ebheke ekukhululekeni kwamasimu wezivini azohlala cishe iminyaka eyi-10. Ukube bekungengenxa yokuthi ukusetshenziswa kwewayini sekunezinkontileka iminyaka embalwa, bekuzobeka amazwe asasebenzisa uhlobo oluthile lokuvikela ukukhiqizwa kwewayini engcupheni ebucayi.

Izimakethe zamafutha omnqumo

Kowoyela ngokujwayelekile izimakethe zehlukile, ngoba uwoyela awusetshenziswanga nje njengomkhiqizo wokudla futhi uthola izicelo kuzimonyo, emithini, emixubeni yezithambisi nakumafutha. Ngokuhlukile ewayinini elinempilo eshalofini ehlukahlukene esukela onyakeni owodwa kuye eminyakeni engama-20 nangaphezulu kuya ngekhwalithi yenqubo yokukhiqiza nokugcina, uwoyela awudluli iminyaka engaphezu kwemibili. Isidingo sento ethile engagcinwa iminyaka eminingi, njengewayini lihlukile, njengoba unemikhiqizo isikhathi eside esinomthelela ekufuneni, kanti i-oyela ingumjikelezo weminyaka emi-2 ojwayelekile.

Incazelo ye- I-EVO uwoyela kunjalo kancane kuqhathaniswa kangcono ne- iwayini njengoba isho inani le-asidi evunyelwe uwoyela ongaba nayo, futhi ngoba uwoyela udinga kuphela ukugcinwa okufanelekile kokongiwa, awutholi ukwelashwa okuningi okwengeziwe njengoba kwenza iwayini ukuzinzisa umkhiqizo wokugcina. Ngoba uwoyela ngumkhiqizo owenziwe ngo-99% wamafutha yemifino, ikhwalithi nezakhiwo zawo kuncike kwiminqumo engavuthiwe (umlimi, ukuvuthwa, impilo) esetshenziselwa ukukhiqizwa, kanye nenqubo yokukhipha (isikhathi selokhu kuvunwa, ukuxova).

Uwoyela njengomkhiqizo "uthembekile" kakhulu uma kuqhathaniswa newayini inqobo nje ukuthi amafutha emifino awakwazi ukukhiqizwa ngokufakelwa. Yize izinhlanganisela zamafutha ezinhlobonhlobo ezejwayelekile zivamile (noma izingxube phakathi kwamafutha omnqumo nembewu), umkhiqizo wokugcina uyabonakala kumafutha enziwe ngo-100% ngeminqumo, ngoba imbewu, amantongomane neminqumo kunokugxila okungafani kokugcwele noma okungagcwalisiwe amafutha ; futhi njengoba uwoyela ungamafutha angama-99%, akunakuba njalo kulungisiwengezithasiselo zangaphandle ezifana newayini ngoba kunzima kakhulu ukudala izinto ezincibilikayo emafutheni. Yini okujwayeleke ukwenziwa ngoyili ukufaka ama-flavour ngenqubo yokunquma (ukunikeza izakhiwo ezinjenge-spiciness, noma ama-flavour angeziwe njenge-lemon, ama-basils, njll.)

Izigayo zikawoyela nezindawo zewayini

Ama-wineries awasakhiqizi iwayini, ayakha kumele bese uthengisa okudingekayo kulowo ofuna ukwenza iwayini, ngoba, njengoba sishilo, iwayini lifana kakhulu futhi ukuphela umkhiqizo kune-a umthombo umkhiqizo. Ama-wineries aqoqa amagilebhisi ezivinini asetha inani ngokuya ngamagilebhisi iziqu ; amasampula we-drill umthwalo bese ulinganisa ukubekwa phansi kwezinto okumele kukhishwe ngesampula lamagilebhisi. Abanye abantu bacabanga ukuthi bangayinyusa i-gradation ngokuxuba ushukela emthwalweni, kepha inqobo nje uma inqubo yenziwa kahle akusizi ngalutho (ngaphandle kokuthi ungafaka umjovo koshukela ngaphakathi amagilebhisi uqobo). Le nqubo ingenziwa yaze yaqina ngokuwasha nangokomisa amagilebhisi ayisampuli ngaphambi kokukhishwa.

Okweminqumo kuyinto yentengo Okuningi engenabulungiswa kuqhathaniswa namagilebhisi. Ayikho inqubo ebeka ikhwalithi, amanani anqunywa ngaphambi kwesandla usuku nosuku . Yini enquma amanani? Kunezici ezimbili eziyinhloko esingazibiza Intengo eyisisekelo futhi Intengo yokusebenza . Intengo eyisisekelo ishiwo yinzuzo encane isigayo samafutha esizimisele ukuyenza enqubweni yokukhiqiza uwoyela. Amanani wokusebenza ashiwo ukuthi isigayo samafutha singakanani uyaxega ngokuba amahora wokufangesikhathi senqubo yokukhiqiza. Njengoba ngesikhathi sokuvuna, isigayo sikawoyela sisebenza ubusuku nemini, kungenxa yomphumela waso omuhle kakhulu wokugcwalisa ulayini wokukhiqiza, ngakho-ke amanani akhula kuphela inqobo nje uma isigayo singenakugcwalisa amahora wokukhiqiza, lokhu kubukeka njengokuhlinzekwa okuyisisekelo nesidingo, kepha kwezolimo unqunyelwe amabanga; lokhu kusho ukuthi ngaphandle kokuthi ungathola ukushoda okuphelele noma umkhiqizo owedlulele, uma ubhekisisa, uzobona ukwehluka kwentengo okungafinyelela ku-3x kuye ku-4x intengo eyisisekelo.

Intengo Bubbles

Uma sithatha ukuthi akekho umlimi ozimisele ukuhambisa umthwalo ngaphezu kwe-50KM evela ezweni lokuvuna lapho-ke singabikezela ukuthi amabhamuza entengo azokwakha azungeze amaqoqo wezigayo zikawoyela (noma amadolobha azungezile, uma kwenzeka kunezindawo ezincane). Abakhiqizi bemihlwathi abangavamile (abangazenzeli owoyela wabo) baba ngaphansi kwanoma iyiphi intengo yemishini yokugaya uwoyela eshoyo, akukho ukuxoxisana, kuyindlela eyodwa yokuhlobana. Uma sicabanga ukuthi kungenzeka lokho amabhizinisi phakathi kwezigayo zika-oyela ezakhelene lapho kungenzeka khona ukuthi umkhiqizi weminqumo angaba lusizi ngokwengeziwe.

Amathuba amabhizinisi phakathi kwezigayo zikawoyela, ngokumangazayo, ayenyuka ngokuncintisana (imincintiswano imvamisa ivimbela ukubuswa ngabodwana). Kanjani? Amabhodlela aphuka lapho izindleko zokuxhumanisaphakathi kwamaqembu kuba phezulu kakhulu. Ukuphakama kwenani labahlanganyeli, kukhuphuka izindleko zokwenza wonke umuntu avumelane ngenqubomgomo efanayo. Kepha lokhu akwenzeki! Awusoze wayibona indawo enezindawo zokugaya uwoyela ezanele zokuphula ama-cartels, ngoba njenganoma yiluphi olunye uhlobo lwesitolo, kuhlale kunezinhlelo ezinhlelweni ezihilelekile ekukhetheni indawo yokugaya okusha. Izwe eliguquka kancane le- izindawo zokuhluza ngemikhiqizo yezolimo kuyenza ibe sesikhathini sezikhathi ezikhona ngokwanele abathengi ukwakha i-cartel futhi akwanele abathengi ukudala ukucindezela komthengi futhi baqale umncintiswano weqiniso. Ngakolunye uhlangothi abalimi baningi (imvamisa baphethe ama-hektare angaphansi kwama-5 emithi yeminqumo), futhi bahlakazeke ngaphesheya kwedolobha elingaphezu kwelilodwa, okwenza izindleko zokudidiyela inhlangano yeziteleka zingavumi. Ngale mithetho, singasho ukuthi ama-cartel maningi amathuba okuthi akhe ezifundeni ezinjenge U-Apulia futhi ESicily uma kuqhathaniswa nezifunda ezisenyakatho ne-Italiya, lapho umkhiqizo uphansi kakhulu futhi ungaphansi.

Le modeli, yamathuba okwakhiwa kwamakhathuni, inganwetshwa kunoma yimuphi umkhiqizo ongena ekuwohlokeni okusheshayo, lapho isikhathi sokucubungula kufanele silandele eduze isikhathi sokuvuna (imfuneko yeminqumo namagilebhisi). Ngenkathi kuvezwa okufana ne- amantongomane angenashell ezingaphatha ezokuthutha zezinsuku eziningi mancane amathuba okuthi zibe namabhamuza entengo. I- ukuhwebelana kwembulunga yonke of kuveza ukufanelekela ukuhamba kwebanga elide, noma kunjalo, kusho ukuthi ukwehluka kwamanani kuzokwehla eminyakeni yonke (intengo izoba ngaphezulu kushintshashintshanjalo ngonyaka), okuzoholela emiphumeleni ebabazekayo emnothweni wendawo okuzodingeka ukuthi umelane neminyaka engabizwanga yokunikezwa ngokweqile ngenxa (ngokwesibonelo) yokutshala ngokugcwele imishini evela emazweni athuthuke kakhulu kwezobuchwepheshe.

Izinkomba Zamanani

Ayikho inkomba yamanani yeminqumo nemivini futhi ngenxa yezizathu ezishiwo ngenhla. Ukuncika kwentengo endaweni kwenza ukuguquguquka kube yinto eyodwa kusho ayilungile, ngoba ayinamandla anele okuchaza. Umlimi ngamunye ubhekene nenhlanganisela yezici ezibandakanya (okuncane) kwesimo sezulu, ukugcinwa kwemboni minyaka yonke, izitayela zemakethe nokuhlanganiswa kwabathengi bendawo.

Ukushintshashintsha okuphezulu kwentengo ngesikhathi sokuvuna nakho kuwubufakazi bokuthi okushayela lo mkhuba akuxhomekile kakhulu kokungaphandle nakwethrendi yezimakethe zomhlaba ezisathonya intengo eyisisekelo, kuyilapho izinto ezincane ezisuselwa endaweni zinesisindo esiphindaphindayo entengweni uqobo.

Noma kunjalo kunokunye. Imivini yamagilebhisi neminqumo ngamafutha a isicelo se-mono uhlobo lwezinto zokusetshenziswa. Lokhu kusho ukuthi kusuka emivinini yamagilebhisi kusetshenziselwa kuphela i-must / iwayini neminqumo kuphela ngamafutha. Azishintshaniswa nezilimo zokusetshenziswa kwetafula. Njengoba umlimi engenabo abanye abathengi abangaba ngabathengi ngaphandle kwewayini nezimboni zikawoyela, kuba ngomunye umkhawulo ezungeze ikhono labalimi lamanani okuxoxisana; akekho omunye umthengi odala ukucindezela komthengi nokuncintisana phakathi kwabathengi.

Okokusebenza okungenzeka ukuthi kunikezwe inkomba yamanani yiwayini namafutha omnqumo, ngoba ichibi labathengi babo likhulu kakhulu ngakho-ke lincintisana ngokweqiniso.

  • Ama-wineries anganquma ukwenza iwayini, noma vele uthengise kumele ngqo kwamanye ama-winery ukuthi angalinganisa isitoreji sawo, nomkhiqizo omusha; kukhona nethuba lokwelula ama-wineries ku- ama-distilleries futhi ukhiqize utshwala ku-marc, bese uguqula i-marc ibe yi-bio-fuel.

  • Amafutha avela ezigayo zikawoyela anezicelo eziningi ezahlukahlukene njengoba kushiwo phambilini (umuthi, izimonyo, okokubasa).

Singafaka isicelo sokwenza okujwayelekile kuleli cala, futhi sithi ukuqina kwamanani abanye okungapheli okuhle kuncike ebuningini bokusebenzisa kwayo. Kubalulekile ukusho ukuthi okuhle Kumele yiba ne- impilo eshalofini , ngoba izinto ezifana nezinsimbi, ezinezindleko zokugcina kanye nezinhlobonhlobo zezicelo, kepha hhayi ukuphela (noma okude kakhulu okungasuswa) kubuswa izisusa ezingeziwe.

Iziphetho

Iqiniso lokuthi inani lentengo yemivini neminqumo alinasisindo endaweni, noma imakethe yomhlaba ibeka abalimi kakhulu isikhundla esiyingozi. Abalimi bakhona vele kuncike esimweni sezulu, kutshala izifo, ukusweleka kwamanzi, okuthi noma kungalawulwa kuze kube sezingeni elithile, kusanikeza ingxenye ayaziwa okuguqukayo kusilinganiso sonyaka senzuzo-ukulahleka okuyi kungezwe intengo akwaziwa . Njengoba izindawo zokuhluza zandisa isethi yamathuluzi ukubhekana nokungaziwa kokukhiqizwa kwempahla eluhlaza, kuba isu elihle kakhulu labalimi, ukuhlanganisa ngokuthe nkqo izinto zokucwenga kumodeli yebhizinisi labo. Ngalesi sikhathi, singacabanga ngemodeli yebhizinisi ebandakanya ubuchwepheshe obukhethekile ekukhiqizweni kwezimpahla ezingavuthiwe ingcwele yesikhathi esedlule, ( isikhathi se-boomer), lapho ukutholakala komhlaba, izimoto nophethiloli kuvumela abalimi ukuthi bathathe izingozi ezinkulu kakhulu namuhla ukuvuthwa ngokwengeziwe izimakethe azisakwazi ukwenzeka.

Isithasiselo

Imishini yokuvuna amagilebhisi

Indlela ejwayeleke kakhulu yokuvuna amagilebhisi sekubambe ngomshini osebenza ngomshini odlula phezu kwemivini yemivini umugqa owodwa ngasikhathi. Le ndlela ivumela izikhathi zokuvuna okusheshayo kepha isivuno ngaphandle kokuqonda amagilebhisi angavuthiwe kulawo avuthiwe. Kodwa-ke izikhathi zokuvuna ezisheshayo zilinganisela ukungakwazi ukushiya amagilebhisi angavuthiwe ngokukhetha isikhathi esifanele sokuvuna ngobunono obukhulu. Le ndlela ingenzeka kuphela ngemipheme ekhule ime mpo, kanti imishini yokulungiselela ukuvuna izindiza ezikhulele phezulu ayikasakazeki, ngoba amasimu anjalo avame kakhulu ezindaweni eziseningizimu, lapho angakha khona isimo sezulu esincanyana esivuna amagilebhisi angaphansi kombhede.

Akukho okuncishisiwe intengo ethonywe izindleko zokusebenza kahle kwaleyo mishini, futhi ngenxa yalokho abalimi abalima amasimu avundlile babekwa engcupheni. Kodwa-ke amagilebhisi avunwa ngesikele agcina izingongolo zamagilebhisi

  • ingabamba iqhaza elibalulekile kunqubo yokuvutshelwa kwewayini

  • yehlisa ukonakala kwamagilebhisi, ivumela ukugcinwa kwamahora angama-48 (lapho kugcinwa emabhokisini amancane), kuvumela ukufinyelela okude kokuhamba.

Lezi zimfanelo ziyenza ikwazi ukuhlukaniswa ngokwanele kusuka kumagilebhisi avunwe ngomshini ukuze kutholakale imali ekhokhwayo ngentengo. Isidingo samagilebhisi asikiwe mancane kodwa asanele ngokwanele, futhi nekusasa lale makethe alaziwa, ngoba liqhutshwa kakhulu ngamakhasimende amancane ukukhiqiza iwayini lawo; umkhuba onjalo ungahle unyamalale kancane selokhu ususelwe emasikweni, noma uzohlala uyindawo yokuzilibazisa engenandawo yokukhula.

Imishini yokuvuna iminqumo

Imishini yokuvuna iminqumo ingukuthuthuka okuvela ekuvunweni kwamagilebhisi okwabelana ngemigomo efanayo futhi kusetshenziswa kwezinye izithelo ezihambisanayo (njengama-alimondi). Zigijima ngemigqa yezitshalo ezincane, zinyakazisa amagatsha ukuvimbela izithelo. Izindlela ezisekelwa ngabantu zokusebenzisa imishini zisabandakanya ukuzamazama, okwenza umkhiqizo ovuniwe ufane, kepha uma kungamagilebhisi, i- ikhwalithi ephezulu umkhiqizo uvunwa ngesandla, ngenxa yeminqumo, nguyena ovunwa ngendlela esebenza ngomshini ngokuphelele, ngoba ngokungabandakanyi ukusetshenziswa amanetha , iminqumo igcina umonakalo omncane womthelela. Ngaphezu kwalokho, i- kakhulu izikhathi zokuvuna ezisheshayo (~ 3x) zivumela inkululeko ethe xaxa ekukhetheni ukuvuthwa okuncanyelwayo, okuphikisana namagilebhisi, ukuvuthwa okuningi akusikho ngokuqinile ikhwalithi engcono, ngoba ukuvuthwa kweminqumo kuyindlela yokukhetha ibhalansi elungile phakathi ikhwalithi (akuvuthiwe kancane) futhi ubuningi (sesivuthiwe).

Amasimu asebenza ngomshini ngokugcwele anomkhawulo wokuthi yiziphi izinhlobo ezingasetshenziswa, kepha lokhu kungumkhawulo wamanje ozoba yinto encane njengoba izinhlobo eziningi zenziwa. Ngaphezu kwalokho kube nemizamo ephumelelayo yokwamukela izinhlobo zemvelo kwezinye izihlahla. Ukuvuna ngomshini noma kunjalo kusagcwele okungaziwa okwenza ikusasa lingaqiniseki:

  • Ukuthenwa ngomshini akulungile, ukuze kutholakale isivuno esihle kufanele kunakekelwe ukuze kuqinisekiswe ukungena kahle, ngenkathi ukuthenwa ngemishini kukwazi ukusebenzisa ukuthena okungaphezulu.

  • Ukutshalwa kunesikhathi sokuphila esingadluli eminyakeni engama-20, okusho ukuthi iminyaka ephakathi kwamasimu iphumela eminyakeni eyishumi. Noma izindleko zokutshala kabusha zingamuncwa njengoba inqubo ingalungiswa, izitshalo ezincane zinobunzima:

    • zidinga ukuchelela okwengeziwe ukugcina izitshalo ziphilile, ngoba izitshalo ezincane azimelani kakhulu nesomiso

    • izitshalo ezincane zingaphansi kokuvunwa okushintshanayo konyaka

    • njengoba izitshalo zomnqumo zincintisana ngokukhanya, ekugcineni ezinye izitshalo zizokhula zide kunezinye, zidale izinkinga ngesikhathi sokuthena nokuvuna

    • izihlahla ezincane zithinteka kalula emakhazeni, kanti isithwathwa sekwedlule isikhathi sesivame kakhulu ngokuguquka kwesimo sezulu

  • Amasimu we-denser athwala ubungozi obukhulu bokungenwa yizifo, okuphenduka izindleko eziphakeme ze-phytopharmaceuticals nokuqapha.

Ukukhetha phakathi ugandaganda usayizi ukusetshenziswa kwemishini kanye usayizi womuntu namanje akucaci, yize kune-R & D eqhubekayo emishinini emikhulu, okunye okwenzekayo ekuvuneni okuncane kungasenza amabhizinisi amancane okulima asebenze ngokuzayo.
