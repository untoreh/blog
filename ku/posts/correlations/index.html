Correlations

A list of correlation metrics

From scipy.stats

  • Pearson (pearsonr)

  • Spearman (spearmanr)

  • Kendall (kendalltau, weightedtau)

  • Theil-Sen (theilslopes)

  • Siegel (siegelslopes)

From Shannon

  • joint entropy

  • conditional entropy

  • information gain

Others

  • quotient correlation ((max(y, x) + max(x, y) - 2) / (max(y, x) * max(x, y) - 1))

  • Cohen (SO)

  • autocorrelation (np.correlate)
