ways of living

A classification of ways of life

This can be defined as a spectrum between builders (settlers) and travelers (nomads).

Builders prefer to be stationary and to accumulate value in its most lasting form, they look for systems and form specialized connections. They enjoy discovery through experimentation.

Travelers prefer not to be stationary and to experience value in its most volatile form, they look for paths, and form broader connections. They enjoy discovery through exploration.
