a jar to strain yogurt

A jar that separates whey from yogurt, for thickening

I haven't found any commercially distributed type of jar with a double bottom such that yogurt can be put from the top, with a cheese cloth like bottom, that strains the yogurt, such that whey can be effortlessly be removed from the bottom of the jar, without the need for an additional decanting step.
